# Chapter 8: Natural Drugs (Plant-Based)

## Cannabis (Marijuana, Hashish, Concentrates)

**Cannabis sativa** is one of the most widely used psychoactive plants:
- **Marijuana**: Dried flower buds of the cannabis plant
- **Hashish**: Compressed resin from cannabis flowers
- **Concentrates**: Extracted THC and other cannabinoids (wax, shatter, oil)
- **Active Compounds**: THC (tetrahydrocannabinol), CBD (cannabidiol)
- **Effects**: Euphoria, relaxation, altered perception, increased appetite
- **Medical Uses**: Pain relief, nausea reduction, appetite stimulation

## Coca (Cocaine, Crack)

**Erythroxylum coca** leaves have been used for thousands of years:
- **Traditional Use**: Chewing leaves for energy and altitude sickness
- **Cocaine**: Extracted alkaloid, white powder stimulant
- **Crack**: Smokable form of cocaine, more rapid onset
- **Effects**: Euphoria, energy, confidence, local anaesthesia
- **Risks**: Cardiovascular problems, addiction, neurological damage

## Opium Poppy (Heroin, Morphine, Codeine)

**Papaver somniferum** produces powerful opioid compounds:
- **Opium**: Dried latex from poppy seed pods
- **Morphine**: Primary active compound, powerful painkiller
- **Heroin**: Semi-synthetic derivative of morphine, more potent
- **Codeine**: Less potent opioid used for pain and cough suppression
- **Effects**: Pain relief, euphoria, sedation, respiratory depression
- **Risks**: High addiction potential, overdose risk, withdrawal severity

## Psilocybin (Magic Mushrooms)

**Psilocybe** mushrooms contain psychedelic compounds:
- **Psilocybin**: Converted to psilocin in the body
- **Effects**: Visual hallucinations, altered perception, spiritual experiences
- **Duration**: 4-6 hours
- **Therapeutic Potential**: Treatment for depression, anxiety, addiction

## Other Natural Drugs (Khat, Ayahuasca, Salvia, Kratom, Kava)

- **Khat**: Stimulant plant used in East Africa and Arabia
- **Ayahuasca**: Amazonian brew containing DMT and MAO inhibitors
- **Salvia**: Divinorum, powerful dissociative hallucinogen
- **Kratom**: Southeast Asian plant with opioid-like effects
- **Kava**: Pacific island plant with mild sedative effects
