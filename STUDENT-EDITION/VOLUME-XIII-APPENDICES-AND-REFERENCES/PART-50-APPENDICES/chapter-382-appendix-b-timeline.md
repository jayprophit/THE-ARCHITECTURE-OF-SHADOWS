# Chapter 382: Appendix B - Timeline

## Historical Timeline of Drug Policy and Trade

**Ancient Period**
- **4000 BCE**: Early evidence of opium use in Sumer
- **2700 BCE**: Chinese emperor Shen Nung mentions cannabis
- **2000 BCE**: Opium cultivation in Egypt
- **1500 BCE**: Use of coca leaves in South America

**19th Century**
- **1806**: Friedrich Serturner isolates morphine
- **1850s**: Opium trade with China expands
- **1856-1860**: Second Opium War
- **1874**: Heroin first synthesized
- **1890s**: Coca-Cola contains cocaine

**Early 20th Century**
- **1909**: Shanghai Opium Commission
- **1912**: Hague International Opium Convention
- **1914**: Harrison Narcotics Tax Act (US)
- **1925**: Geneva Convention on opium
- **1930s**: US Federal Bureau of Narcotics established

**Mid 20th Century**
- **1948**: UN Commission on Narcotic Drugs
- **1961**: UN Single Convention on Narcotic Drugs
- **1971**: UN Convention on Psychotropic Substances
- **1971**: Nixon declares War on Drugs
- **1973**: DEA established

**Late 20th Century**
- **1988**: UN Convention Against Illicit Traffic
- **1980s**: Crack epidemic in US
- **1990s**: Rise of Colombian cartels
- **1996**: California medical cannabis legalization
- **1998**: UN General Assembly Special Session on drugs

**21st Century**
- **2001**: Portugal decriminalizes drugs
- **2006**: Mexico's war on cartels begins
- **2012**: Colorado and Washington legalize cannabis
- **2014**: First US states legalize recreational cannabis
- **2016**: UN General Assembly Special Session on drugs
- **2018**: Canada legalizes cannabis
- **2020s**: Psychedelic renaissance begins
- **2020s**: Fentanyl crisis escalates

**Note**: This is a selective timeline highlighting key events.
