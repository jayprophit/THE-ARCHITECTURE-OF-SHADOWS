# Chapter 384: Appendix D - Organizations

## Key Organizations in Drug Policy and Trade

**International Organizations**
- **UNODC**: United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime
- **INCB**: International Narcotics Control Board
- **WHO**: World Health Organization
- **EMCDDA**: European Monitoring Centre for Drugs and Drug Addiction
- **OAS**: Organization of American States (CICAD)
- **Interpol**: International Criminal Police Organization

**US Government Agencies**
- **DEA**: Drug Enforcement Administration
- **FDA**: Food and Drug Administration
- **NIH**: National Institutes of Health (NIDA)
- **ONDCP**: Office of National Drug Control Policy
- **CDC**: Centers for Disease Control and Prevention
- **SAMHSA**: Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration

**International Law Enforcement**
- **Europol**: European Union Agency for Law Enforcement Cooperation
- **DEA International**: DEA international offices
- **Joint Operations**: Various international joint operations

**Non-Governmental Organizations**
- **Drug Policy Alliance**: US drug policy reform organization
- **Transform Drug Policy Foundation**: UK drug policy reform
- **Release**: UK drug information and advice
- **Harm Reduction International**: Global harm reduction advocacy
- **International Drug Policy Consortium**: Global network of NGOs

**Think Tanks and Research**
- **RAND Corporation**: Policy research organization
- **Brookings Institution**: Public policy research
- **CATO Institute**: Libertarian think tank
- **Various Academic Centers**: University-based drug policy centers

**Note**: This is a selective list of key organizations.
