# Chapter 110: The Packaging

## Presentation and Distribution

Packaging serves multiple purposes in the drug trade:

**Packaging Functions:**
- **Protection**: Protecting drugs from damage and contamination
- **Concealment**: Concealing drugs from detection
- **Measurement**: Pre-measured doses for sale
- **Branding**: Branding for market recognition
- **Information**: Sometimes includes dosage or product information
- **Distribution**: Facilitating distribution and sale

**Types of Packaging:**
- **Street-Level**: Small plastic bags, foil wraps, capsules
- **Wholesale**: Larger packages for wholesale distribution
- **Branded**: Branded packaging for market recognition
- **Counterfeit**: Counterfeit pharmaceutical packaging
- **Professional**: Professional-looking packaging
- **Custom**: Custom packaging for specific markets

**Packaging Materials:**
- **Plastic Bags**: Small plastic bags for street-level sales
- **Foil**: Aluminum foil wraps
- **Capsules**: Empty capsules for powder drugs
- **Blister Packs**: Counterfeit blister packs
- **Bottles**: Bottles for pills and liquids
- **Vacuum-Sealed**: Vacuum-sealed for preservation

**Branding and Marketing:**
- **Logos**: Logos stamped on pills
- **Colours**: Colour coding for different products
- **Names**: Brand names for drug products
- **Reputation**: Building reputation for quality
- **Counterfeiting**: Counterfeiting popular brands
- **Marketing**: Marketing through packaging

**Packaging and Detection:**
- **Concealment**: Packaging designed to avoid detection
- **Disguise**: Packaging disguised as legitimate products
- **Odour Masking**: Packaging to mask drug odour
- **X-Ray Evasion**: Packaging to evade X-ray detection
- **Canine Evasion**: Packaging to evade canine detection
- **Innovation**: Constant innovation in packaging

**The Packaging Reality:**
- **Professional**: Increasingly professional packaging
- **Sophisticated**: Sophisticated packaging methods
- **Marketing**: Packaging as marketing tool
- **Detection Challenge**: Packaging challenges detection
- **Consumer Appeal**: Packaging appeals to consumers
- **Innovation**: Constant innovation in packaging
