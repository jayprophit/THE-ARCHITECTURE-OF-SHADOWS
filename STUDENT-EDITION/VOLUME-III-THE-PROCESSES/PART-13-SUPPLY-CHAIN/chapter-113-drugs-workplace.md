# Chapter 113: Drugs in the Workplace

## Substance Use in Employment

Drug use in the workplace affects productivity and safety:

**Types of Workplace Drug Use:**
- **Recreational Use**: Recreational drug use affecting work
- **Functional Use**: Using drugs to enhance work performance
- **Self-Medication**: Self-medicating for work-related stress
- **Addiction**: Addiction affecting work performance
- **Prescription Use**: Prescription drug use affecting work
- **Alcohol Use**: Alcohol use affecting work

**Affected Industries:**
- **Construction**: High rates of drug use in construction
- **Transportation**: Drug use in transportation industries
- **Healthcare**: Drug use in healthcare professions
- **Hospitality**: Drug use in hospitality industry
- **Manufacturing**: Drug use in manufacturing
- **Professional Services**: Drug use in professional services

**Consequences:**
- **Productivity**: Reduced productivity
- **Safety**: Safety risks from impaired workers
- **Accidents**: Increased workplace accidents
- **Absenteeism**: Increased absenteeism
- **Turnover**: Increased staff turnover
- **Liability**: Employer liability for drug-related incidents

**Testing and Policies:**
- **Drug Testing**: Workplace drug testing programs
- **Random Testing**: Random drug testing
- **Pre-Employment Testing**: Pre-employment drug screening
- **Reasonable Suspicion**: Testing based on reasonable suspicion
- **Post-Accident Testing**: Testing after workplace accidents
- **Drug-Free Workplace Policies**: Drug-free workplace policies

**Treatment and Support:**
- **Employee Assistance Programs**: EAPs for drug issues
- **Treatment Referrals**: Referrals to treatment programs
- **Return to Work**: Return to work programs after treatment
- **Accommodation**: Accommodation for prescription drug use
- **Support**: Support for employees with drug issues
- **Privacy**: Privacy protections for employees

**The Workplace Drug Reality:**
- **Widespread**: Widespread drug use in workplaces
- **Economic Impact**: Significant economic impact
- **Safety Risk**: Significant safety risks
- **Complex**: Complex issue requiring balanced approach
- **Testing Controversy**: Controversy over drug testing
- **Treatment vs. Punishment**: Tension between treatment and punishment approaches
