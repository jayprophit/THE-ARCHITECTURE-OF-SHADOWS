# Chapter 116: Drugs in Conflict Zones

## Drug Trade in Areas of Conflict

Drug trade thrives in conflict zones:

**Characteristics of Conflict Zone Drug Trade:**
- **State Weakness**: Weak or absent state authority
- **Lawlessness**: Lawlessness enables drug trade
- **Armed Groups**: Armed groups involved in drug trade
- **Strategic Location**: Strategic location for trafficking routes
- **Economic Necessity**: Drug trade as economic necessity
- **Funding Conflict**: Drug trade funding armed conflict

**Examples:**
- **Afghanistan**: Opium production funding conflict
- **Myanmar**: Opium and methamphetamine production in conflict zones
- **Colombia**: Coca production funding armed groups
- **Mexico**: Drug violence and state weakness
- **West Africa**: Cocaine transit in weak states
- **Golden Triangle**: Historic conflict zone drug production

**Armed Group Involvement:**
- **Funding**: Drug trade funding armed groups
- **Taxation**: Taxation of drug trade
- **Protection**: Providing protection for drug operations
- **Direct Involvement**: Direct involvement in drug production and trafficking
- **Territorial Control**: Control of drug-producing territories
- **Strategic Alliances**: Strategic alliances with drug traffickers

**Impact on Conflict:**
- **Funding**: Drug trade funding conflict
- **Prolonging**: Prolonging conflicts through drug revenue
- **Corruption**: Corruption of institutions
- **Violence**: Drug-related violence
- **State Fragility**: Contributing to state fragility
- **Post-Conflict**: Drug trade continuing after conflict

**International Response:**
- **Counter-Narcotics**: Counter-narcotics operations
- **Counter-Terrorism**: Counter-terrorism operations
- **Stabilisation**: Stabilisation efforts
- **Development**: Development programs to reduce drug cultivation
- **Alternative Livelihoods**: Alternative livelihood programs
- **Security**: Security operations against drug trade

**The Conflict Zone Drug Reality:**
- **Symbiotic**: Symbiotic relationship between conflict and drugs
- **Complex**: Complex dynamics between armed groups and drug trade
- **Persistent**: Persistent problem in conflict zones
- **Challenging**: Challenging to address
- **Integrated**: Integrated with conflict dynamics
- **Post-Conflict**: Often continues after conflict resolution
