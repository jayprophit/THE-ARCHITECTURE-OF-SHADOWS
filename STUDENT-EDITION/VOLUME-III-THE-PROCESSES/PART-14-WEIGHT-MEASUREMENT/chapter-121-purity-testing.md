# Chapter 121: Purity Testing

## Determining Drug Purity

Purity testing determines the quality and safety of drugs:

**Testing Methods:**
- **Reagent Testing**: Chemical reagent tests for substance identification
- **Colorimetric Tests**: Colour-changing tests for substance identification
- **Thin Layer Chromatography (TLC)**: Laboratory testing method
- **Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (GC-MS)**: Advanced laboratory testing
- **High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC)**: Advanced laboratory testing
- **FTIR Spectroscopy**: Spectroscopic analysis

**Reagent Testing Kits:**
- **Marquis Reagent**: Tests for MDMA, amphetamines, opioids
- **Mecke Reagent**: Tests for MDMA, opioids
- **Mandelin Reagent**: Tests for MDMA, ketamine, cocaine
- **Simon's Reagent**: Distinguishes MDMA from MDA
- **Froehde Reagent**: Tests for LSD, 2C-B
- **Liebermann Reagent**: Tests for various substances

**Harm Reduction Services:**
- **Drug Checking Services**: Services that test drugs for users
- **Festival Testing**: Drug testing at festivals and events
- **Fixed Sites**: Fixed-site drug checking services
- **Mobile Services**: Mobile drug checking services
- **Online Services**: Online drug testing services
- **Community Organisations**: Community organisations providing testing

**Challenges:**
- **Access**: Limited access to testing services
- **Legal Status**: Legal status of drug testing services
- **Cost**: Cost of testing services
- **Accuracy**: Variable accuracy of testing methods
- **Substance Identification**: Difficulty identifying novel substances
- **Purity Measurement**: Difficulty measuring precise purity

**The Purity Testing Reality:**
- **Harm Reduction**: Critical harm reduction tool
- **Limited Access**: Limited access for most users
- **Variable Accuracy**: Variable accuracy of methods
- **Legal Barriers**: Legal barriers to widespread testing
- **Growing Acceptance**: Growing acceptance of drug checking
- **Life-Saving**: Can be life-saving
