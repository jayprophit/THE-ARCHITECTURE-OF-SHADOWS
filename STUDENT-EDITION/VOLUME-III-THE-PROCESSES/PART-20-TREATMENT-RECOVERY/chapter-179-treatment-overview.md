# Chapter 179: Treatment Overview

## Drug Treatment Approaches

Drug treatment encompasses various approaches to addiction:

**Treatment Approaches:**
- **Detoxification**: Medical detoxification from drugs
- **Inpatient Treatment**: Residential inpatient treatment
- **Outpatient Treatment**: Outpatient treatment programs
- **Medication-Assisted Treatment**: Medication-assisted treatment (MAT)
- **Behavioural Therapy**: Behavioural therapy approaches
- **Support Groups**: Support groups and peer support

**Detoxification:**
- **Medical Detox**: Medically supervised detoxification
- **Withdrawal Management**: Management of withdrawal symptoms
- **Inpatient Detox**: Inpatient detoxification programs
- **Outpatient Detox**: Outpatient detoxification programs
- **Duration**: Variable duration depending on substance
- **Medical Supervision**: Medical supervision for safety

**Inpatient Treatment:**
- **Residential Programs**: Residential treatment programs
- **Therapeutic Communities**: Therapeutic community programs
- **Duration**: Variable duration (30 days to several months)
- **Structure**: Structured daily programs
- **Therapy**: Individual and group therapy
- **Aftercare**: Aftercare planning

**Outpatient Treatment:**
- **Intensive Outpatient**: Intensive outpatient programs
- **Standard Outpatient**: Standard outpatient programs
- **Day Programs**: Day treatment programs
- **Flexibility**: Flexible scheduling
- **Living at Home**: Patients live at home during treatment
- **Continuum of Care**: Part of continuum of care

**The Treatment Reality:**
- **Varied Approaches**: Varied approaches to treatment
- **Individual Variation**: Individual variation in treatment needs
- **Evidence-Based**: Evidence-based approaches most effective
- **Access Barriers**: Barriers to treatment access
- **Stigma**: Stigma affecting treatment seeking
- **Recovery Possible**: Recovery is possible with appropriate treatment
