# Chapter 136: Detection and Prevention

## Combating Money Laundering

Detection and prevention efforts target money laundering:

**Detection Methods:**
- **Suspicious Activity Reports**: Financial institutions file SARs
- **Currency Transaction Reports**: Reports of large cash transactions
- **Financial Intelligence**: Financial intelligence units analyse data
- **Blockchain Analysis**: Analysis of cryptocurrency transactions
- **Pattern Recognition**: Pattern recognition algorithms
- **Whistleblowers**: Whistleblower reports

**Prevention Measures:**
- **AML Regulations**: Anti-money laundering regulations
- **KYC Requirements**: Know-your-customer requirements
- **Customer Due Diligence**: Enhanced due diligence for high-risk customers
- **Transaction Monitoring**: Monitoring of suspicious transactions
- **Staff Training**: Training staff to recognise money laundering
- **Internal Controls**: Internal controls to prevent money laundering

**International Cooperation:**
- **FATF**: Financial Action Task Force sets standards
- **Egmont Group**: Network of financial intelligence units
- **UNODC**: UN Office on Drugs and Crime
- **Interpol**: International police cooperation
- **Regional Bodies**: Regional AML bodies
- **Information Sharing**: International information sharing

**Challenges:**
- **Complexity**: Complexity of money laundering methods
- **International Scope**: International scope complicates enforcement
- **Resource Constraints**: Limited resources for comprehensive enforcement
- **Professional Expertise**: Money launderers have professional expertise
- **Adaptive Methods**: Money launderers constantly adapt
- **Political Will**: Political will for enforcement varies

**The Detection Prevention Reality:**
- **Constant Battle**: Constant battle against money laundering
- **Resource Intensive**: Resource intensive to detect and prevent
- **International**: Requires international cooperation
- **Adaptive**: Must adapt to evolving methods
- **Limited Success**: Limited success in preventing money laundering
- **Systemic Challenge**: Systemic challenge requiring systemic solutions
