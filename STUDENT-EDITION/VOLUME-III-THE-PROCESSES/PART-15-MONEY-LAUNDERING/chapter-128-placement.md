# Chapter 128: Placement

## Introducing Illicit Cash into the Financial System

Placement is the first stage of money laundering:

**Placement Methods:**
- **Cash Deposits**: Depositing cash in bank accounts
- **Cash Businesses**: Using cash-intensive businesses to deposit money
- **Smurfing**: Breaking large cash amounts into small deposits
- **Currency Smuggling**: Physically moving cash across borders
- **Money Mules**: Using individuals to move money
- **Purchase of Assets**: Purchasing assets with cash

**Cash Business Placement:**
- **Restaurants**: Restaurants as cash businesses
- **Bars and Clubs**: Bars and clubs as cash businesses
- **Retail Stores**: Retail stores as cash businesses
- **Service Businesses**: Service businesses as cash businesses
- **Vending Machines**: Vending machines for cash placement
- **Casinos**: Casinos as cash businesses

**Smurfing:**
- **Structured Deposits**: Structured deposits below reporting thresholds
- **Multiple Accounts**: Using multiple accounts for deposits
- **Multiple Individuals**: Using multiple individuals for deposits
- **Geographic Spread**: Depositing across multiple geographic locations
- **Time Spread**: Depositing over extended time periods
- **Detection Challenges**: Challenging to detect smurfing

**Currency Smuggling:**
- **Physical Transport**: Physically transporting cash across borders
- **Concealment**: Concealing cash in luggage, vehicles, or on person
- **Commercial Shipments**: Hiding cash in commercial shipments
- **Private Aircraft**: Using private aircraft to transport cash
- **Maritime Transport**: Using maritime transport for cash
- **Detection**: Detection of currency smuggling

**The Placement Reality:**
- **Riskiest Stage**: Riskiest stage of money laundering
- **Cash Intensive**: Requires handling large amounts of cash
- **Detection Risk**: High risk of detection at this stage
- **Essential**: Essential for money laundering process
- **Adaptive**: Constantly adapting to detection methods
- **Vulnerable**: Vulnerable to interdiction
