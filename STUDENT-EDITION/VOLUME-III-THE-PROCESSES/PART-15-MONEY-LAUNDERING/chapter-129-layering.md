# Chapter 129: Layering

## Obscuring the Money Trail

Layering creates complex transaction trails to obscure money origins:

**Layering Methods:**
- **Wire Transfers**: Multiple wire transfers between accounts
- **Shell Companies**: Using shell companies to move money
- **Offshore Accounts**: Moving money to offshore jurisdictions
- **Investments**: Investing in legitimate businesses
- **Complex Transactions**: Complex financial transactions
- **International Movement**: Moving money across international borders

**Wire Transfer Layering:**
- **Multiple Transfers**: Multiple transfers between accounts
- **International Transfers**: International wire transfers
- **Shell Company Transfers**: Transfers between shell companies
- **Timing**: Careful timing of transfers
- **Amount Structuring**: Structuring amounts to avoid detection
- **Geographic Spread**: Transfers across multiple jurisdictions

**Shell Company Layering:**
- **Corporate Structures**: Complex corporate structures
- **Offshore Companies**: Offshore shell companies
- **Nominee Directors**: Nominee directors hiding ownership
- **Bearer Shares**: Bearer shares hiding ownership
- **Trust Structures**: Trust structures hiding ownership
- **Multiple Jurisdictions**: Companies in multiple jurisdictions

**Investment Layering:**
- **Stock Market**: Buying and selling stocks
- **Real Estate**: Buying and selling real estate
- **Business Investments**: Investing in legitimate businesses
- **Art and Antiques**: Buying and selling art and antiques
- **Precious Metals**: Buying and selling precious metals
- **Cryptocurrency**: Using cryptocurrency for layering

**The Layering Reality:**
- **Complex**: Extremely complex transaction trails
- **International**: International scope complicates investigation
- **Professional**: Professional expertise required
- **Expensive**: Expensive to implement
- **Effective**: Effective at obscuring money trails
- **Detection Challenge**: Significant challenge for detection
