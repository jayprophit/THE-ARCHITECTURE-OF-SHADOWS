# Chapter 144: Risks for Dark Web Users

## Dangers of Buying Drugs Online

Dark web drug users face significant risks:

**Legal Risks:**
- **Arrest**: Risk of arrest for purchasing illegal drugs
- **Package Interception**: Risk of packages being intercepted by customs
- **Law Enforcement Monitoring**: Law enforcement monitoring of markets
- **Address Recording**: Address recorded in market databases
- **Digital Footprint**: Digital footprint from transactions
- **Prosecution**: Prosecution for drug possession

**Scam Risks:**
- **Exit Scams**: Markets exit scamming users
- **Vendor Scams**: Vendors not delivering products
- **Quality Scams**: Products not as advertised
- **Money Loss**: Loss of cryptocurrency through scams
- **No Recourse**: Limited recourse for scams
- **Fake Markets**: Fake markets stealing credentials

**Health Risks:**
- **Unknown Quality**: Unknown quality and purity of products
- **Unknown Substances**: Risk of unknown substances
- **Fentanyl Contamination**: Risk of fentanyl contamination
- **No Testing**: No drug testing before purchase
- **Dosage Uncertainty**: Uncertainty about dosage
- **Health Consequences**: Potential health consequences

**Security Risks:**
- **Identity Exposure**: Risk of identity exposure
- **Address Exposure**: Address exposure through deliveries
- **Digital Security**: Digital security risks
- **Phishing**: Phishing attacks on market users
- **Malware**: Malware targeting dark web users
- **Account Compromise**: Account compromise risks

**The User Risk Reality:**
- **Significant**: Significant risks for dark web users
- **Legal**: Legal risks from purchasing illegal drugs
- **Scam**: High risk of scams
- **Health**: Health risks from unknown products
- **Security**: Security risks from anonymous transactions
- **Calculated Risk**: Users take calculated risks
