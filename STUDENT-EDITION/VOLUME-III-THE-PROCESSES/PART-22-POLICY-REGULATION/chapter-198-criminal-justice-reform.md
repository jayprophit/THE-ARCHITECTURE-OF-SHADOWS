# Chapter 198: Criminal Justice Reform

## Reforming Drug Enforcement and Sentencing

Criminal justice reform addresses the failures of drug enforcement:

**Sentencing Reform:**
- **Mandatory Minimum Reform**: Reform or elimination of mandatory minimums
- **Sentencing Guidelines**: Reform of sentencing guidelines
- **Racial Disparities**: Addressing racial disparities in sentencing
- **Drug Courts**: Drug courts as alternative to incarceration
- **Diversion Programs**: Diversion programs for non-violent offenders
- **Early Release**: Early release for non-violent drug offenders

**Decriminalisation:**
- **Personal Possession**: Decriminalisation of personal possession
- **Civil Penalties**: Civil instead of criminal penalties
- **Focus on Treatment**: Focus on treatment over punishment
- **Reduced Arrests**: Reduced arrests for drug offenses
- **Record Expungement**: Expungement of drug convictions
- **Social Justice**: Social justice considerations

**Drug Courts:**
- **Treatment Instead of Incarceration**: Treatment instead of incarceration
- **Judicial Supervision**: Judicial supervision of treatment
- **Multidisciplinary**: Multidisciplinary approach
- **Graduated Sanctions**: Graduated sanctions for non-compliance
- **Success Rates**: Success rates of drug courts
- **Cost-Effectiveness**: Cost-effectiveness compared to incarceration

**Prison Reform:**
- **Treatment in Prison**: Drug treatment in prisons
- **Reentry Programs**: Reentry programs for formerly incarcerated
- **Reducing Recidivism**: Programs to reduce recidivism
- **Prison Conditions**: Improving prison conditions
- **Alternatives to Incarceration**: Alternatives to incarceration
- **Sentencing Reform**: Sentencing reform to reduce prison population

**The Criminal Justice Reform Reality:**
- **Bipartisan Support**: Growing bipartisan support for reform
- **Evidence-Based**: Evidence-based reforms
- **Cost Considerations**: Cost considerations driving reform
- **Social Justice**: Social justice driving reform
- **State-Level Innovation**: State-level innovation in reform
- **Federal Reform**: Federal reform efforts
