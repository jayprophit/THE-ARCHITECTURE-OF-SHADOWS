# Chapter 199: Policy Evaluation

## Assessing Drug Policy Effectiveness

Evaluating drug policy effectiveness is complex but essential:

**Evaluation Metrics:**
- **Drug Use Prevalence**: Changes in drug use prevalence
- **Drug Availability**: Changes in drug availability
- **Drug Prices**: Changes in drug prices
- **Drug Purity**: Changes in drug purity
- **Overdose Deaths**: Changes in overdose deaths
- **Treatment Admissions**: Changes in treatment admissions

**Health Metrics:**
- **Mortality**: Drug-related mortality
- **Morbidity**: Drug-related morbidity
- **Infectious Disease**: Rates of infectious disease
- **Hospital Admissions**: Drug-related hospital admissions
- **Emergency Department Visits**: Drug-related ED visits
- **Birth Outcomes**: Drug-related birth outcomes

**Social Metrics:**
- **Crime Rates**: Drug-related crime rates
- **Incarceration Rates**: Drug-related incarceration rates
- **Family Stability**: Impact on family stability
- **Employment**: Impact on employment
- **Education**: Impact on education
- **Community Well-Being**: Impact on community well-being

**Economic Metrics:**
- **Cost of Enforcement**: Cost of drug enforcement
- **Cost of Treatment**: Cost of drug treatment
- **Healthcare Costs**: Drug-related healthcare costs
- **Productivity Loss**: Productivity losses from drug use
- **Tax Revenue**: Tax revenue from regulated markets
- **Cost-Benefit Analysis**: Cost-benefit analysis of policies

**Challenges:**
- **Causality**: Difficulty establishing causality
- **Time Lags**: Time lags between policy and outcomes
- **Multiple Factors**: Multiple factors affecting outcomes
- **Data Quality**: Data quality issues
- **Confounding Variables**: Confounding variables
- **Political Influence**: Political influence on evaluation

**The Policy Evaluation Reality:**
- **Complex**: Complex evaluation process
- **Essential**: Essential for evidence-based policy
- **Contested**: Contested interpretations of data
- **Political**: Political considerations affect evaluation
- **Evidence-Based**: Need for evidence-based policy
- **Ongoing**: Ongoing evaluation needed
