# Chapter 197: Harm Reduction Policy

## Policy Approaches to Reducing Harm

Harm reduction policy focuses on reducing the negative consequences of drug use:

**Policy Principles:**
- **Non-Judgmental**: Non-judgmental approach to drug use
- **User-Centred**: Centred on the needs of drug users
- **Pragmatic**: Pragmatic approach focused on reducing harm
- **Evidence-Based**: Evidence-based interventions
- **Human Rights**: Respect for human rights
- **Access**: Access to services without barriers

**Syringe Exchange Policy:**
- **Legal Framework**: Legal framework for syringe exchange
- **Funding**: Funding for syringe exchange programs
- **Access**: Access to syringe exchange services
- **Pharmacy Access**: Pharmacy access to syringes
- **Decriminalisation**: Decriminalisation of syringe possession
- **Public Health**: Public health justification for programs

**Supervised Consumption Site Policy:**
- **Legal Framework**: Legal framework for consumption sites
- **Funding**: Funding for supervised consumption sites
- **Community Engagement**: Community engagement in site location
- **Regulatory Approval**: Regulatory approval for sites
- **Political Support**: Political support for sites
- **Public Health**: Public health justification for sites

**Naloxone Access Policy:**
- **Over-the-Counter**: Over-the-counter naloxone access
- **Pharmacy Access**: Pharmacy access to naloxone
- **Standing Orders**: Standing orders for naloxone distribution
- **Good Samaritan Laws**: Good Samaritan laws protecting overdose responders
- **Training**: Training programs for naloxone administration
- **Insurance Coverage**: Insurance coverage for naloxone

**Drug Checking Policy:**
- **Legal Framework**: Legal framework for drug checking
- **Funding**: Funding for drug checking services
- **Regulatory Approval**: Regulatory approval for services
- **Quality Standards**: Quality standards for drug checking
- **Liability**: Liability protections for drug checking services
- **Integration**: Integration with other harm reduction services

**The Harm Reduction Policy Reality:**
- **Evidence-Based**: Strong evidence base for effectiveness
- **Controversial**: Controversial despite evidence
- **Expanding**: Expanding acceptance and implementation
- **Political**: Political considerations affect implementation
- **Public Health**: Public health focus of policies
- **Life-Saving**: Life-saving policy interventions
