# Chapter 194: Prescription Drug Regulation

## Regulating Pharmaceutical Drugs

Prescription drug regulation controls access to pharmaceutical substances:

**Regulatory Framework:**
- **FDA Approval**: FDA approval process for medications
- **Prescription Requirements**: Prescription-only access
- **Controlled Substances**: Controlled substances regulation
- **Scheduling**: Scheduling of prescription medications
- **Manufacturing Regulation**: Regulation of pharmaceutical manufacturing
- **Distribution Regulation**: Regulation of drug distribution

**Prescription Monitoring:**
- **PDMPs**: Prescription Drug Monitoring Programs
- **Doctor Shopping**: Detection of doctor shopping
- **Overprescribing**: Monitoring for overprescribing
- **Pharmacy Monitoring**: Pharmacy monitoring systems
- **Data Sharing**: Data sharing between states
- **Privacy Concerns**: Privacy concerns about monitoring

**Opioid Regulation:**
- **Opioid Prescribing Guidelines**: Guidelines for opioid prescribing
- **Prescription Limits**: Limits on opioid prescriptions
- **Risk Evaluation and Mitigation Strategies (REMS)**: REMS for opioids
- **Opioid Crisis Response**: Response to opioid crisis
- **Pain Management**: Balance between pain management and regulation
- **Access Concerns**: Concerns about patient access to pain medication

**Stimulant Regulation:**
- **ADHD Medication**: Regulation of ADHD medications
- **Prescription Requirements**: Strict prescription requirements
- **Monitoring**: Monitoring of stimulant prescriptions
- **Diversion Prevention**: Prevention of diversion
- **Access Concerns**: Access concerns for patients with ADHD
- **Abuse Potential**: Abuse potential of prescription stimulants

**The Prescription Regulation Reality:**
- **Balancing Act**: Balance between access and control
- **Crisis Response**: Response to prescription drug crisis
- **Monitoring**: Increasing monitoring of prescriptions
- **Patient Access**: Concerns about patient access
- **Diversion**: Significant diversion of prescription drugs
- **Evolving**: Evolving regulatory approaches
