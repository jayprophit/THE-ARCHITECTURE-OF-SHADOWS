# Chapter 174: Arrests and Prosecutions

## Legal Action Against Drug Offenders

Arrests and prosecutions target individuals involved in the drug trade:

**Arrest Strategies:**
- **Street-Level Arrests**: Arrests of street-level dealers
- **Mid-Level Arrests**: Arrests of mid-level traffickers
- **High-Level Arrests**: Arrests of high-level traffickers
- **Kingpin Arrests**: Arrests of drug kingpins
- **International Arrests**: International arrests and extraditions
- **Coordinated Operations**: Coordinated multi-agency operations

**Prosecution Strategies:**
- **Drug Charges**: Drug possession and trafficking charges
- **Conspiracy Charges**: Conspiracy charges for drug organisations
- **RICO Charges**: RICO charges for organised crime
- **Money Laundering Charges**: Money laundering charges
- **Asset Forfeiture**: Asset forfeiture alongside criminal charges
- **Cooperation Deals**: Cooperation deals for reduced sentences

**Sentencing:**
- **Mandatory Minimums**: Mandatory minimum sentences
- **Sentencing Guidelines**: Sentencing guidelines for drug offenses
- **Enhanced Sentences**: Enhanced sentences for aggravating factors
- **Three Strikes**: Three-strikes laws for repeat offenders
- **Federal vs. State**: Federal vs. state sentencing
- **International Variation**: International variation in sentencing

**Challenges:**
- **Prison Overcrowding**: Prison overcrowding from drug arrests
- **Racial Disparities**: Racial disparities in arrests and sentencing
- **Cost**: High cost of incarceration
- **Rehabilitation**: Limited rehabilitation in prison
- **Recidivism**: High recidivism rates
- **Effectiveness**: Questions about effectiveness

**The Arrest Prosecution Reality:**
- **Massive Scale**: Massive scale of arrests and prosecutions
- **Costly**: Extremely costly to criminal justice system
- **Racial Disparities**: Significant racial disparities
- **Limited Effectiveness**: Limited effectiveness in reducing drug use
- **Prison Industrial Complex**: Growth of prison industrial complex
- **Need for Reform**: Need for sentencing reform
