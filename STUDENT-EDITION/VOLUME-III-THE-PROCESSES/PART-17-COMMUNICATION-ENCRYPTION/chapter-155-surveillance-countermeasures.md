# Chapter 155: Surveillance and Countermeasures

## Detecting and Evading Surveillance

Drug trade participants must detect and evade surveillance:

**Types of Surveillance:**
- **Physical Surveillance**: Physical following and observation
- **Electronic Surveillance**: Electronic monitoring (wiretaps, GPS, etc.)
- **Communications Surveillance**: Monitoring of communications
- **Financial Surveillance**: Monitoring of financial transactions
- **Cyber Surveillance**: Monitoring of online activities
- **Aerial Surveillance**: Aerial surveillance (drones, helicopters)

**Surveillance Detection:**
- **Route Analysis**: Analysis of travel routes for surveillance
- **Behavioural Analysis**: Analysis of behaviour for surveillance indicators
- **Environmental Awareness**: Awareness of environment for surveillance
- **Counter-Surveillance Routes**: Counter-surveillance driving/walking routes
- **Time Variation**: Variation of timing to detect surveillance
- **Technical Detection**: Technical detection of electronic surveillance

**Countermeasures:**
- **Evasion**: Evasion of surveillance through route and timing
- **Disguise**: Disguise to avoid identification
- **Decoy**: Decoy operations to distract surveillance
- **Technical Countermeasures**: Technical countermeasures against electronic surveillance
- **Compartmentalisation**: Compartmentalisation to limit surveillance impact
- **Contingency Plans**: Contingency plans for surveillance detection

**Electronic Surveillance Countermeasures:**
- **Encryption**: Encryption of communications
- **Signal Detection**: Detection of tracking devices
- **Device Sweeping**: Sweeping for listening devices
- **Faraday Cages**: Faraday cages to block signals
- **Secure Devices**: Use of secure devices
- **Regular Rotation**: Regular rotation of devices

**The Surveillance Reality:**
- **Constant Threat**: Constant threat of surveillance
- **Sophisticated**: Increasingly sophisticated surveillance methods
- **Resource Intensive**: Resource intensive to evade
- **Professional**: Professional surveillance requires professional countermeasures
- **Cat and Mouse**: Constant cat and mouse with law enforcement
- **Risk**: High risk of detection
