# Chapter 307: Post-Conflict Drug Challenges

## Drug Issues After Armed Conflict

Drug challenges persist and often worsen after armed conflict ends:

**Post-Conflict Economies:**
- **Drug Economies**: Persistence of drug economies
- **Lack of Alternatives**: Lack of economic alternatives
- **Unemployment**: High unemployment driving drug trade
- **Infrastructure**: Destroyed infrastructure
- **Governance**: Weak governance enabling drug trade
- **Development Challenges**: Development challenges

**Veteran Issues:**
- **PTSD**: PTSD among veterans
- **Addiction**: Addiction among veterans
- **Unemployment**: Veteran unemployment
- **Reintegration**: Reintegration challenges
- **Treatment Access**: Limited treatment access
- **Social Problems**: Social problems among veterans

**Criminal Groups:**
- **Paramilitaries**: Paramilitaries transitioning to criminal groups
- **Cartels**: Cartels emerging from conflict
- **Armed Groups**: Armed groups becoming criminal organizations
- **Violence**: Continued violence
- **Territorial Control**: Territorial control by criminal groups
- **Corruption**: Corruption enabling criminal groups

**Peace Process Challenges:**
- **DDR**: Disarmament, Demobilization, Reintegration challenges
- **Drug Trade in Peace Process**: Drug trade complicating peace process
- **Power Sharing**: Power sharing with drug-funded groups
- **Justice**: Justice for drug-related crimes
- **Economic Reintegration**: Economic reintegration challenges
- **Sustainable Peace**: Sustainable peace challenges

**International Response:**
- **Post-Conflict Assistance**: International post-conflict assistance
- **Alternative Livelihoods**: Alternative livelihood programs
- **Treatment**: Treatment programs for veterans and communities
- **Rule of Law**: Rule of law strengthening
- **Development**: Development assistance
- **Coordination**: Coordination challenges

**The Post-Conflict Reality:**
- **Challenging**: Challenging post-conflict environment
- **Persistent**: Persistent drug challenges
- **Veteran Crisis**: Veteran crisis
- **Criminal Evolution**: Evolution of armed groups into criminal organizations
- **Development Need**: Need for development assistance
- **Sustainable Solutions**: Need for sustainable solutions
