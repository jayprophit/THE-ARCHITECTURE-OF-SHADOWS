# Chapter 215: Dental Health

## Oral Health Consequences of Drug Use

Drug use has significant impacts on dental and oral health:

**Methamphetamine and Dental Health:**
- **Meth Mouth**: Severe dental decay from methamphetamine use
- **Tooth Decay**: Rapid tooth decay
- **Gum Disease**: Severe gum disease
- **Tooth Loss**: Tooth loss from meth use
- **Dry Mouth**: Xerostomia contributing to decay
- **Teeth Grinding**: Bruxism damaging teeth

**Opioids and Dental Health:**
- **Dry Mouth**: Opioid-induced dry mouth
- **Tooth Decay**: Increased tooth decay risk
- **Gum Disease**: Increased gum disease risk
- **Poor Oral Hygiene**: Neglect of oral hygiene
- **Sugar Cravings**: Sugar cravings contributing to decay
- **Dental Neglect**: Dental neglect among opioid users

**Cannabis and Dental Health:**
- **Dry Mouth**: Cannabis-induced dry mouth
- **Gum Disease**: Increased gum disease risk
- **Oral Cancer**: Potential oral cancer risk
- **Periodontal Disease**: Periodontal disease risk
- **Poor Oral Hygiene**: Neglect of oral hygiene
- **Staining**: Tooth staining from cannabis smoke

**Alcohol and Dental Health:**
- **Tooth Decay**: Alcohol contributing to tooth decay
- **Gum Disease**: Increased gum disease risk
- **Oral Cancer**: Increased oral cancer risk
- **Erosion**: Acid erosion from alcohol
- **Dry Mouth**: Alcohol-induced dry mouth
- **Poor Oral Hygiene**: Neglect of oral hygiene

**Prevention and Treatment:**
- **Oral Hygiene**: Importance of oral hygiene
- **Dental Care**: Regular dental care
- **Dry Mouth Management**: Management of dry mouth
- **Sugar Reduction**: Reducing sugar intake
- **Fluoride**: Fluoride treatments
- **Education**: Education about oral health

**The Dental Health Reality:**
- **Significant Impact**: Significant impact on oral health
- **Preventable**: Many effects preventable
- **Treatment**: Treatment can address many issues
- **Neglect**: Dental neglect common among drug users
- **Stigma**: Stigma affecting dental care seeking
- **Public Health**: Oral health as public health issue
