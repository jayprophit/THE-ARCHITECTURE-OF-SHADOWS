# Chapter 219: Healthcare Costs

## Economic Costs of Drug-Related Health Issues

Drug use imposes significant costs on healthcare systems:

**Direct Healthcare Costs:**
- **Emergency Department Visits**: Emergency department visits for drug-related issues
- **Hospitalizations**: Hospitalizations for drug-related conditions
- **Treatment Programs**: Costs of addiction treatment programs
- **Medication-Assisted Treatment**: Costs of MAT medications
- **Physician Services**: Physician services for drug-related issues
- **Mental Health Services**: Mental health services for co-occurring disorders

**Indirect Healthcare Costs:**
- **Chronic Disease Management**: Management of drug-related chronic diseases
- **Infectious Disease Treatment**: Treatment of infectious diseases
- **Pregnancy and Neonatal Care**: Costs of pregnancy complications and NAS
- **Long-Term Care**: Long-term care for drug-related disabilities
- **Preventive Care**: Preventive care costs
- **Pharmaceutical Costs**: Pharmaceutical costs for related conditions

**Emergency Services:**
- **Emergency Medical Services**: EMS responses to overdoses
- **Overdose Reversal**: Costs of naloxone and emergency response
- **Trauma Care**: Trauma care for drug-related injuries
- **Psychiatric Emergencies**: Psychiatric emergency services
- **Critical Care**: Critical care for severe overdoses
- **Transportation**: Medical transportation costs

**Insurance Costs:**
- **Premium Increases**: Increased insurance premiums
- **Claims Costs**: Drug-related insurance claims
- **Medicaid Costs**: Medicaid costs for drug-related care
- **Medicare Costs**: Medicare costs for drug-related care
- **Private Insurance**: Private insurance costs
- **Uncompensated Care**: Uncompensated care costs

**The Healthcare Cost Reality:**
- **Significant**: Significant economic burden
- **Preventable**: Many costs preventable
- **Growing**: Growing healthcare costs
- **System Impact**: Significant impact on healthcare systems
- **Cost-Effective**: Prevention and treatment cost-effective
- **Public Investment**: Public investment in prevention saves money
