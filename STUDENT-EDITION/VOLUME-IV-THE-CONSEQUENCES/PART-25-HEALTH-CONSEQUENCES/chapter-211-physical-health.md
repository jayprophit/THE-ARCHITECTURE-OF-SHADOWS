# Chapter 211: Physical Health Consequences

## Physical Health Effects of Drug Use

Drug use has widespread effects on physical health:

**Cardiovascular Effects:**
- **Heart Disease**: Increased risk of heart disease
- **Heart Attack**: Increased risk of heart attack
- **Stroke**: Increased risk of stroke
- **Arrhythmias**: Heart rhythm abnormalities
- **Hypertension**: High blood pressure
- **Cardiomyopathy**: Damage to heart muscle

**Respiratory Effects:**
- **Lung Damage**: Lung damage from smoking drugs
- **Respiratory Infections**: Increased respiratory infections
- **Chronic Bronchitis**: Chronic bronchitis from smoking
- **Emphysema**: Emphysema from smoking
- **Lung Cancer**: Lung cancer risk
- **Asthma**: Exacerbation of asthma

**Liver Damage:**
- **Hepatitis**: Viral hepatitis from injection drug use
- **Cirrhosis**: Liver cirrhosis from alcohol
- **Liver Failure**: Liver failure from various substances
- **Toxic Hepatitis**: Toxic hepatitis from substances
- **Liver Cancer**: Increased liver cancer risk
- **Fatty Liver**: Fatty liver disease

**Kidney Damage:**
- **Kidney Failure**: Kidney failure from substances
- **Nephrotoxicity**: Toxic effects on kidneys
- **Dehydration**: Kidney damage from dehydration
- **Rhabdomyolysis**: Muscle breakdown affecting kidneys
- **Chronic Kidney Disease**: Chronic kidney disease
- **Kidney Stones**: Increased kidney stone risk

**Gastrointestinal Effects:**
- **Gastrointestinal Damage**: Damage to GI tract
- **Ulcers**: Ulcers from substance use
- **Malnutrition**: Malnutrition from chronic use
- **Appetite Changes**: Appetite changes affecting nutrition
- **Digestive Problems**: Various digestive problems
- **Pancreatitis**: Pancreatitis from alcohol

**The Physical Health Reality:**
- **Widespread**: Widespread physical health effects
- **Organ Damage**: Damage to multiple organ systems
- **Preventable**: Many effects preventable
- **Treatment**: Treatment can mitigate some effects
- **Recovery**: Some damage reversible with abstinence
- **Public Health**: Major public health concern
