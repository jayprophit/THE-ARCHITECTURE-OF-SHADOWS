# Chapter 223: Education Impact

## The Effect of Drug Use on Education

Drug use significantly impacts educational outcomes:

**Student Drug Use:**
- **Academic Performance**: Decreased academic performance
- **Attendance**: Poor school attendance
- **Dropout Rates**: Increased dropout rates
- **Behavioral Problems**: Behavioral problems in school
- **Cognitive Function**: Impaired cognitive function affecting learning
- **Future Opportunities**: Limited future educational opportunities

**School Environment:**
- **Drug Dealing**: Drug dealing in and around schools
- **Drug Use On Campus**: Drug use on school grounds
- **Violence**: Drug-related violence in schools
- **Safety Concerns**: Safety concerns for students and staff
- **School Climate**: Negative impact on school climate
- **Resource Diversion**: School resources diverted to drug issues

**Teacher Impact:**
- **Classroom Management**: Challenges in classroom management
- **Stress**: Increased stress for teachers
- **Safety**: Safety concerns for teachers
- **Time**: Time spent on drug-related issues
- **Burnout**: Teacher burnout
- **Professional Development**: Need for training on drug issues

**Family Impact on Education:**
- **Parental Drug Use**: Impact of parental drug use on children's education
- **Home Environment**: Unstable home environment affecting education
- **Support**: Lack of parental support for education
- **Financial Strain**: Financial strain affecting educational resources
- **Stress**: Stress affecting children's educational performance
- **Intergenerational**: Intergenerational educational impact

**Prevention and Intervention:**
- **School-Based Prevention**: School-based prevention programs
- **Counseling Services**: School counseling services
- **Early Intervention**: Early intervention for at-risk students
- **Parental Involvement**: Parental involvement in prevention
- **Community Partnerships**: Partnerships with community organizations
- **Alternative Education**: Alternative education for at-risk youth

**The Education Impact Reality:**
- **Significant**: Significant impact on educational outcomes
- **Long-Term**: Long-term consequences for life opportunities
- **Preventable**: Many impacts preventable through intervention
- **Systemic**: Systemic issues affecting education
- **Investment Needed**: Investment in prevention and intervention
- **Equity Concerns**: Disproportionate impact on disadvantaged students
