# Chapter 38: The Trafficker

## International Smuggling

Traffickers move drugs across international borders:

**Methods of Transportation:**
- **Commercial Shipping**: Hidden in cargo containers
- **Air Transport**: Concealed in luggage, cargo, or body
- **Land Transport**: Hidden in vehicles, on persons, or in goods
- **Maritime**: Private boats, fishing vessels, submarines
- **Postal Services**: Packages sent through mail systems

**Smuggling Techniques:**
- **Concealment**: Hidden in legitimate goods
- **Body Packing**: Swallowing or inserting drug packets
- **False Compartments**: Secret compartments in vehicles
- **Dissolving**: Drugs dissolved in liquids
- **Replication**: Drugs made to look like other products

**Routes:**
- **Established Routes**: Well-known trafficking corridors
- **New Routes**: Constantly developing new paths
- **Multi-Modal**: Combination of air, sea, and land transport
- **Transit Countries**: Countries used as transit points
- **Direct Routes**: Direct from producer to consumer countries

## The Scale of Operations

**Volume and Value:**
- **Multi-Ton Shipments**: Large-scale shipments worth millions
- **Regular Shipments**: Constant flow of drugs across borders
- **Global Network**: Operations spanning multiple continents
- **Sophisticated Logistics**: Complex supply chain management
- **Investment**: Significant investment in infrastructure

**Organisation:**
- **Criminal Organisations**: Often part of larger criminal networks
- **Specialisation**: Specialised roles within trafficking organisations
- **Coordination**: Complex coordination across multiple countries
- **Technology**: Use of advanced technology for logistics and security
- **Corruption**: Corruption facilitates trafficking operations

**Risks:**
- **Interdiction**: Risk of seizure by law enforcement
- **Arrest**: Risk of arrest and prosecution
- **Violence**: Risk of violence from competitors or enforcement
- **Financial Loss**: Loss of valuable shipments
- **Long Sentences**: Harsh penalties for trafficking

**The Trafficker's Role:**
- **Critical Link**: Essential for international drug trade
- **High Risk**: High risk but high reward
- **Specialised Skills**: Requires specialised knowledge and skills
- **Global Impact**: Operations affect multiple countries
- **Law Enforcement Priority**: Primary target of international law enforcement
