# Chapter 68: The "Conservative"

## Advocating for Strict Drug Enforcement

Conservatives advocate for strict drug enforcement:

**Roles and Responsibilities:**
- **Advocacy**: Advocating for strict drug enforcement
- **Public Statements**: Making public statements supporting prohibition
- **Policy Development**: Developing tough-on-drugs policies
- **Political Support**: Supporting politicians who support prohibition
- **Media Engagement**: Engaging with media on drug issues
- **Community Organising**: Organising community opposition to drug liberalisation

**Conservative Approaches:**
- **Prohibition**: Maintaining strict prohibition
- **Enforcement**: Emphasis on law enforcement
- **Deterrence**: Belief in deterrence through harsh penalties
- **Zero Tolerance**: Zero tolerance approach to drug use
- **Family Values**: Framing drug policy in terms of family values
- **Public Safety**: Emphasis on public safety

**Conservative Organisations:**
- **Law Enforcement Organisations**: Police and prison organisations
- **Religious Groups**: Religious organisations opposing drug liberalisation
- **Parent Groups**: Parent organisations concerned about drugs
- **Community Groups**: Community groups opposing drug liberalisation
- **Think Tanks**: Conservative think tanks supporting prohibition
- **Political Organisations**: Conservative political organisations

**Challenges:**
- **Evidence Contradiction**: Evidence often contradicts prohibition approach
- **Public Opinion**: Shifting public opinion on drug policy
- **International Trends**: International trend toward reform
- **Resource Constraints**: High cost of enforcement approach
- **Effectiveness Questions**: Questions about effectiveness of prohibition
- **Human Rights Concerns**: Human rights concerns about harsh enforcement

**The Conservative's Perspective:**
- **Public Safety**: Concern for public safety
- **Moral Values**: Moral opposition to drug use
- **Family Protection**: Desire to protect families
- **Community Values**: Concern for community values
- **Traditional Values**: Commitment to traditional values
