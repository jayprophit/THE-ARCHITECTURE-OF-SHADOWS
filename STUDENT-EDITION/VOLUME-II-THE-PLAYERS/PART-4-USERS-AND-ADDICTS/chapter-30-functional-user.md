# Chapter 30: The "Functional" User

## Holding Down a Job While Using

Functional users maintain employment despite regular drug use:

**Characteristics:**
- **Employment**: Maintains steady job, often successful
- **Responsibilities**: Meets work and family obligations
- **Secret Use**: Drug use often hidden from colleagues and family
- **Controlled Pattern**: Use organised around work schedule
- **Appearance**: May appear normal to others

**Common Patterns:**
- **After Work**: Use after completing work responsibilities
- **Weekend Use**: Heavy use on weekends, sober during week
- **Maintenance Use**: Just enough to function, not to get high
- **Prescription Use**: Legitimate prescriptions used inappropriately

**Substances Commonly Used by Functional Users:**
- **Alcohol**: Socially acceptable, easy to hide
- **Cocaine**: Perceived as performance-enhancing by some
- **Prescription Drugs**: Legitimate sources, harder to detect
- **Cannabis**: Increasing social acceptance
- **Stimulants**: Used for work performance

## The Risks of Functioning

Functional use carries significant risks:

**Health Risks:**
- **Cumulative Damage**: Long-term health effects accumulate
- **Hidden Problems**: Health issues may go undetected
- **Tolerance**: Increasing doses needed for same effect
- **Withdrawal**: May experience withdrawal without recognising it

**Psychological Risks:**
- **Denial**: Belief that use is under control
- **Isolation**: Secret use prevents honest relationships
- **Stress**: Maintaining the facade is stressful
- **Progression**: Can transition to non-functional use

**Social Risks:**
- **Relationship Damage**: Secret use damages trust
- **Legal Risks**: Possession and use remain illegal
- **Professional Risks**: Discovery could end career
- **Financial Costs**: Drug use is expensive

**The "Functioning" Illusion:**
- **Not Truly Functional**: Performance often subtly impaired
- **Cost of Maintenance**: Significant energy devoted to maintaining appearance
- **Eventual Decline**: Most functional users eventually decline
- **Intervention Needed**: Often requires crisis to seek help
