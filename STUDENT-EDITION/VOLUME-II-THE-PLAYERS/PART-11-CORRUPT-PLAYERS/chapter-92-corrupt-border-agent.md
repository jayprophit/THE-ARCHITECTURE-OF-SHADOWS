# Chapter 92: The Corrupt Border Agent

## Border Security Corruption

Corrupt border agents facilitate drug trafficking across borders:

**Forms of Border Corruption:**
- **Bribery**: Accepting bribes to allow drug shipments
- **Selective Inspection**: Selectively inspecting shipments
- **Information Leaks**: Leaking inspection schedules and methods
- **Fast-Tracking**: Fast-tracking shipments through inspection
- **Document Forgery**: Forging or falsifying documents
- **Smuggling Assistance**: Direct assistance in smuggling operations

**Mechanisms of Corruption:**
- **Cash Payments**: Direct cash payments for allowing shipments
- **Regular Payments**: Regular payments for ongoing cooperation
- **Family Benefits**: Benefits to family members
- **Coercion**: Coercion by criminal organisations
- **Network Corruption**: Networks of corrupt border agents
- **International Coordination**: Coordination with corrupt agents in other countries

**Impact on Border Security:**
- **Erosion of Security**: Erosion of border security
- **Increased Trafficking**: Increased drug trafficking
- **Public Safety Risk**: Public safety risk from increased drug supply
- **Economic Impact**: Economic impact of increased drug trade
- **International Consequences**: International consequences of border corruption
- **Security Breaches**: Security breaches beyond drug trafficking

**Detection Challenges:**
- **Volume**: High volume of legitimate shipments hides corruption
- **Professional Expertise**: Professional expertise in avoiding detection
- **Network Protection**: Networks protect corrupt agents
- **International Scope**: International scope complicates investigation
- **Resource Constraints**: Limited resources for comprehensive screening
- **Legal Complexity**: Legal complexity of border operations

**The Corrupt Border Agent's Reality:**
- **Power**: Border power enables corruption
- **Rationalisation**: Rationalisation of corrupt behaviour
- **Risk**: Risk of exposure and prosecution
- **Professional Betrayal**: Betrayal of professional duty
- **Systemic Impact**: Systemic impact on border security
- **Accountability**: Need for border security accountability
