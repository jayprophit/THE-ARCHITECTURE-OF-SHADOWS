# Chapter 95: The "Organised Crime" Player

## Organised Crime Networks

Organised crime players operate sophisticated criminal enterprises:

**Organised Crime Characteristics:**
- **Hierarchical Structure**: Organised hierarchical structures
- **Specialised Roles**: Specialised roles within organisations
- **Violence**: Use of violence to maintain control
- **Corruption**: Systematic corruption of institutions
- **International Operations**: International criminal operations
- **Diversification**: Diversification into multiple criminal activities

**Types of Organised Crime:**
- **Cartels**: Drug cartels (Sinaloa, CJNG, etc.)
- **Mafia**: Traditional mafia organisations
- **Gangs**: Street gangs and criminal gangs
- **Triads**: Chinese organised crime
- **Yakuza**: Japanese organised crime
- **Russian Organised Crime**: Russian criminal organisations

**Business Model:**
- **Vertical Integration**: Control of entire supply chain
- **Territorial Control**: Control of specific territories
- **Market Monopolies**: Monopolies on specific markets
- **Diversification**: Diversification into other criminal activities
- **Legitimate Business**: Legitimate businesses as cover
- **Political Influence**: Political influence and corruption

**Violence and Enforcement:**
- **Territorial Violence**: Violence to maintain territories
- **Internal Discipline**: Violent internal discipline
- **Intimidation**: Intimidation of communities and competitors
- **Assassinations**: Targeted assassinations of rivals
- **Public Violence**: Public displays of violence
- **Torture**: Torture as enforcement tool

**Corruption and Influence:**
- **Political Corruption**: Corruption of politicians
- **Law Enforcement Corruption**: Corruption of law enforcement
- **Judicial Corruption**: Corruption of judiciary
- **Banking Corruption**: Corruption of banking system
- **Media Influence**: Influence over media
- **Social Influence**: Influence over communities

**The Organised Crime Player's Reality:**
- **Power**: Significant power in territories
- **Violence**: Constant violence and threat
- **Wealth**: Significant wealth from criminal activities
- **Risk**: Constant risk of arrest and violence
- **Complexity**: Complex criminal operations
- **Systemic Impact**: Systemic impact on society
