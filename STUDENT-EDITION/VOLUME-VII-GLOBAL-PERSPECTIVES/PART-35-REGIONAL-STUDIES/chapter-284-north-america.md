# Chapter 284: North America

## Drug Issues in North America

North America is a major consumer and producer region:

**United States:**
- **Consumer Market**: Largest consumer market for illicit drugs
- **Opioid Crisis**: Devastating opioid crisis
- **Cannabis Legalisation**: State-level cannabis legalisation
- **Enforcement**: Significant drug enforcement apparatus
- **Treatment**: Treatment system challenges
- **Policy Debates**: Active policy debates

**Canada:**
- **Cannabis Legalisation**: First G7 country to legalise cannabis
- **Opioid Crisis**: Significant opioid crisis
- **Harm Reduction**: Strong harm reduction approach
- **Enforcement**: Different enforcement approach than US
- **Treatment**: Universal healthcare treatment access
- **Policy**: Progressive drug policy

**Mexico:**
- **Producer Country**: Major producer of heroin, methamphetamine, cannabis
- **Transit Country**: Major transit country to US
- **Cartels**: Powerful drug cartels
- **Violence**: Extreme drug-related violence
- **US Influence**: Strong US influence on policy
- **Policy Challenges**: Significant policy challenges

**North American Drug Trade:**
- **US-Mexico Trade**: Drug trade across US-Mexico border
- **Canada-US Trade**: Drug trade between Canada and US
- **Production**: Production in Mexico and Canada
- **Consumption**: Consumption primarily in US
- **Enforcement**: Enforcement cooperation and tensions
- **Policy Coordination**: Policy coordination challenges

**The North America Reality:**
- **Major Consumer**: Major consumer region
- **Producer**: Significant production in Mexico
- **Crisis**: Ongoing opioid crisis
- **Policy Innovation**: Policy innovation in cannabis
- **Violence**: Significant violence in Mexico
- **Cooperation**: Cooperation and tensions between countries
