# Chapter 241: Decriminalisation Overview

## The Decriminalisation Alternative

Decriminalisation represents a middle ground between prohibition and legalisation:

**Decriminalisation Defined:**
- **Definition**: Removal of criminal penalties for personal possession
- **Civil Penalties**: Civil instead of criminal penalties
- **Focus on Treatment**: Focus on treatment rather than punishment
- **Reduced Enforcement**: Reduced law enforcement emphasis
- **Distinction from Legalisation**: Distinction from full legalisation
- **Variation**: Variation in decriminalisation models

**Decriminalisation Models:**
- **Civil Fines**: Civil fines instead of criminal charges
- **Administrative Penalties**: Administrative penalties for possession
- **Diversion to Treatment**: Diversion to treatment instead of prosecution
- **Thresholds**: Quantity thresholds for decriminalisation
- **Drug-Specific**: Drug-specific decriminalisation
- **Comprehensive**: Comprehensive decriminalisation of all drugs

**Arguments for Decriminalisation:**
- **Reduced Criminal Justice Impact**: Reduced criminal justice system involvement
- **Cost Savings**: Cost savings from reduced incarceration
- **Racial Justice**: Reduction in racial disparities
- **Treatment Focus**: Emphasis on treatment over punishment
- **Stigma Reduction**: Reduced stigma for drug users
- **Evidence-Based**: Evidence from successful implementations

**Arguments Against Decriminalisation:**
- **Increased Use**: Potential for increased drug use
- **Mixed Message**: Mixed message about drug acceptability
- **Enforcement Challenges**: Challenges in enforcement
- **Public Concerns**: Public concerns about drug use
- **Implementation Issues**: Implementation challenges
- **Limited Impact**: Limited impact on drug markets

**The Decriminalisation Reality:**
- **Evidence-Based**: Strong evidence base from implementations
- **Effective**: Effective at reducing criminal justice impact
- **Controversial**: Controversial despite evidence
- **Growing Support**: Growing support for decriminalisation
- **Middle Ground**: Middle ground between prohibition and legalisation
- **Policy Option**: Viable policy option for reform
