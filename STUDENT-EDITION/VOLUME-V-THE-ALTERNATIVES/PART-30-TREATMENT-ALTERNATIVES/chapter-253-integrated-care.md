# Chapter 253: Integrated Care

## Coordinated Healthcare for Drug Users

Integrated care provides coordinated healthcare for people who use drugs:

**Integrated Care Model:**
- **Coordination**: Coordination between healthcare providers
- **Comprehensive**: Comprehensive addressing of health needs
- **Patient-Centred**: Patient-centred care approach
- **Continuity**: Continuity of care across settings
- **Collaboration**: Collaboration between providers
- **Holistic**: Holistic approach to health

**Service Integration:**
- **Primary Care**: Integration with primary healthcare
- **Mental Health**: Integration with mental health services
- **Addiction Treatment**: Integration with addiction treatment
- **Social Services coordination**: Coordination with social services
- **Specialty Care**: Coordination with specialty care
- **Community Resources**: Connection to community resources

**Benefits of Integrated Care:**
- **Improved Outcomes**: Improved health outcomes
- **Reduced Fragmentation**: Reduced fragmentation of care
- **Increased Engagement**: Increased patient engagement
- **Cost Savings**: Cost savings from coordinated care
- **Better Experience**: Better patient experience
- **Prevention**: Prevention of health problems

**Implementation Challenges:**
- **Funding**: Funding for integrated care models
- **Workforce**: Need for trained workforce
- **Communication**: Communication between providers
- **Data Sharing**: Data sharing between systems
- **Organizational Barriers**: Organizational barriers to integration
- **Reimbursement**: Reimbursement models for integrated care

**Models of Integration:**
- **Co-Location**: Co-location of services
- **Care Management**: Care management approaches
- **Health Homes**: Health home models
- **Accountable Care**: Accountable care organizations
- **Community Health Centers**: Community health center integration
- **Specialty Clinics**: Specialty integrated clinics

**The Integrated Care Reality:**
- **Effective**: Effective at improving outcomes
- **Challenging**: Challenging to implement
- **Essential**: Essential for comprehensive care
- **Evidence-Based**: Evidence-based approach
- **Cost-Effective**: Cost-effective over time
- **Future**: Future of healthcare for drug users
