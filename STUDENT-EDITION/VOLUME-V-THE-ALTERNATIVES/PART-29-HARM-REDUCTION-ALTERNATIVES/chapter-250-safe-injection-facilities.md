# Chapter 250: Safe Injection Facilities

## Comprehensive Harm Reduction Centers

Safe injection facilities provide comprehensive harm reduction services:

**Facility Overview:**
- **Supervised Injection**: Supervised injection spaces
- **Medical Supervision**: Medical staff on-site
- **Sterile Equipment**: Provision of sterile injection equipment
- **Overdose Response**: Immediate overdose response
- **Health Services**: Comprehensive health services
- **Social Services**: Social support services

**Service Components:**
- **Injection Rooms**: Supervised injection rooms
- **Smoking Rooms**: Rooms for smoking drugs
- **Equipment Provision**: Sterile equipment provision
- **Naloxone**: Immediate naloxone access
- **Primary Care**: Primary healthcare services
- **Mental Health**: Mental health services
- **Addiction Treatment**: Addiction treatment services
