# Chapter 264: Public Opinion on Drug Policy

## Attitudes Toward Drug Policy Approaches

Public opinion on drug policy varies across different approaches:

**Enforcement Approaches:**
- **Support for Enforcement**: Support for law enforcement approaches
- **Harsh Penalties**: Support for harsh penalties
- **Drug Courts**: Support for drug courts
- **Mandatory Minimums**: Mixed support for mandatory minimums
- **Decriminalisation**: Growing support for decriminalisation
- **Legalisation**: Growing support for legalisation

**Treatment Approaches:**
- **Treatment Support**: Strong support for treatment approaches
- **Insurance Coverage**: Support for insurance coverage of treatment
- **Medication-Assisted Treatment**: Support for MAT
- **Prevention Programs**: Support for prevention programs
- **Recovery Support**: Support for recovery support services
- **Treatment Over Punishment**: Preference for treatment over punishment

**Harm Reduction Approaches:**
- **Syringe Exchange**: Growing support for syringe exchange
- **Supervised Consumption**: Mixed support for supervised consumption sites
- **Naloxone Access**: Strong support for naloxone access
- **Drug Checking**: Limited awareness and support
- **Harm Reduction Philosophy**: Growing support for harm reduction philosophy
- **Controversy**: Controversy around some harm reduction approaches

**Policy Priorities:**
- **Public Health**: Public health as priority
- **Public Safety**: Public safety as priority
- **Cost**: Cost considerations in policy support
- **Effectiveness**: Effectiveness influencing support
- **Social Justice**: Social justice considerations
- **Individual Liberty**: Individual liberty considerations

**The Policy Opinion Reality:**
- **Complex**: Complex and varied opinions
- **Evolving**: Evolving support for different approaches
- **Context-Dependent**: Opinions depend on context
- **Evidence-Based**: Evidence influencing opinions
- **Divided**: Divided opinions on some approaches
- **Influential**: Influencing policy debates
