# Chapter 260: Media Literacy

## Critical Analysis of Drug Media

Media literacy enables critical analysis of drug-related media:

**Media Literacy Skills:**
- **Critical Analysis**: Critical analysis of media messages
- **Source Evaluation**: Evaluation of media sources
- **Bias Detection**: Detection of bias in media coverage
- **Fact-Checking**: Fact-checking media claims
- **Context Understanding**: Understanding context of media coverage
- **Perspective Recognition**: Recognition of different perspectives

**Analyzing News Coverage:**
- **Framing Analysis**: Analysis of how issues are framed
- **Source Evaluation**: Evaluation of sources used in coverage
- **Bias Detection**: Detection of bias in reporting
- **Sensationalism Recognition**: Recognition of sensationalist coverage
- **Contextual Understanding**: Understanding broader context
- **Multiple Perspectives**: Seeking multiple perspectives

**Analyzing Entertainment Media:**
- **Stereotype Recognition**: Recognition of stereotypes
- **Realism Assessment**: Assessment of realism in portrayals
- **Impact Analysis**: Analysis of impact on perceptions
- **Glamourisation Recognition**: Recognition of glamorisation
- **Consequences Depiction**: Analysis of consequence depiction
- **Representation Analysis**: Analysis of representation

**Analyzing Social Media:**
- **Source Verification**: Verification of social media sources
- **Misinformation Detection**: Detection of misinformation
- **Algorithm Understanding**: Understanding of social media algorithms
- **Echo Chamber Recognition**: Recognition of echo chambers
- **Influence Analysis**: Analysis of influencer influence
- **Platform Understanding**: Understanding platform dynamics

**Teaching Media Literacy:**
- **Education Programs**: Media literacy education programs
- **School Curriculum**: Integration into school curriculum
- **Parent Education**: Parent education on media literacy
- **Community Programs**: Community-based media literacy programs
- **Online Resources**: Online media literacy resources
- **Professional Development**: Professional development for educators

**The Media Literacy Reality:**
- **Essential**: Essential skill in modern media environment
- **Developing**: Field of media literacy developing
- **Educational**: Educational approaches to media literacy
- **Critical**: Critical thinking about media
- **Empowering**: Empowering individuals to navigate media
- **Necessary**: Necessary for informed citizenship
