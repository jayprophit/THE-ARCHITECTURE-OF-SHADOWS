# Chapter 226: Economic Impact

## The Economic Costs of Drug Use

Drug use imposes significant economic costs on society:

**Direct Economic Costs:**
- **Healthcare Costs**: Healthcare costs for drug-related issues
- **Law Enforcement Costs**: Costs of drug law enforcement
- **Incarceration Costs**: Costs of incarceration for drug offenses
- **Treatment Costs**: Costs of drug treatment programs
- **Prevention Costs**: Costs of prevention programs
- **Judicial Costs**: Judicial system costs for drug cases

**Indirect Economic Costs:**
- **Lost Productivity**: Lost productivity from drug use
- **Absenteeism**: Absenteeism from drug use
- **Premature Death**: Economic loss from premature death
- **Disability**: Economic costs of drug-related disability
- **Reduced Earnings**: Reduced lifetime earnings
- **Unemployment**: Unemployment related to drug use

**Crime Costs:**
- **Property Loss**: Losses from drug-related property crime
- **Victim Costs**: Costs to victims of drug-related crime
- **Insurance Costs**: Increased insurance premiums
- **Security Costs**: Increased security costs
- **Justice System Costs**: Justice system costs
- **Correctional Costs**: Correctional system costs

**Social Welfare Costs:**
- **Social Services**: Social services costs
- **Child Welfare**: Child welfare costs
- **Disability Benefits**: Disability benefit costs
- **Unemployment Benefits**: Unemployment benefit costs
- **Housing Assistance**: Housing assistance costs
- **Other Benefits**: Other social welfare costs

**Economic Benefits of Illicit Market:**
- **Illicit Economy**: Size of illicit drug economy
- **Employment**: Employment in illicit drug trade
- **Profits**: Profits from drug trade
- **Money Laundering**: Economic activity from money laundering
- **Corruption**: Economic costs of corruption
- **Distortion**: Distortion of legitimate economies

**The Economic Impact Reality:**
- **Massive**: Massive economic costs
- **Preventable**: Many costs preventable through policy change
- **Cost-Effective**: Prevention and treatment cost-effective
- **Policy-Dependent**: Economic impact depends on policy
- **Reform Potential**: Policy reform can reduce economic costs
- **Complex**: Complex economic relationships
