# Chapter 222: Community Impact

## The Effect of Drug Use on Communities

Drug use has significant impacts on communities:

**Social Impact:**
- **Social Cohesion**: Erosion of social cohesion
- **Trust**: Decreased trust in communities
- **Community Safety**: Decreased sense of safety
- **Social Networks**: Disruption of social networks
- **Community Institutions**: Strain on community institutions
- **Social Capital**: Loss of social capital

**Economic Impact:**
- **Property Values**: Decline in property values
- **Business Impact**: Negative impact on local businesses
- **Employment**: Impact on local employment
- **Tourism**: Negative impact on tourism
- **Economic Development**: Hindered economic development
- **Tax Base**: Reduced tax base

**Public Space Impact:**
- **Public Drug Use**: Public drug use in community spaces
- **Discarded Paraphernalia**: Discarded drug paraphernalia
- **Homelessness**: Homelessness and drug use
- **Public Nuisance**: Public nuisance from drug activity
- **Vandalism**: Increased vandalism
- **Litter**: Increased litter

**Violence Impact:**
- **Drug Violence**: Drug-related violence in communities
- **Gang Activity**: Gang activity affecting communities
- **Fear**: Community fear of violence
- **Police Presence**: Increased police presence
- **Trauma**: Community trauma from violence
- **Security Costs**: Increased security costs

**Children and Youth:**
- **Exposure**: Children exposed to drug activity
- **Recruitment**: Youth recruitment into drug trade
- **School Impact**: Impact on schools
- **After-School Programs**: Impact on after-school programs
- **Youth Services**: Strain on youth services
- **Future Generations**: Impact on future generations

**The Community Impact Reality:**
- **Widespread**: Widespread impact on communities
- **Disproportionate**: Disproportionate impact on vulnerable communities
- **Long-Term**: Long-term consequences
- **Recovery Possible**: Community recovery possible
- **Investment Needed**: Community investment needed
- **Systemic**: Systemic issues requiring systemic solutions
