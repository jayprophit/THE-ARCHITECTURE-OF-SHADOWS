# Chapter 224: Workplace Impact

## The Effect of Drug Use on Employment

Drug use has significant impacts on workplaces and employment:

**Employee Drug Use:**
- **Productivity**: Decreased productivity
- **Absenteeism**: Increased absenteeism
- **Presenteeism**: Presenteeism (present but not productive)
- **Accidents**: Increased workplace accidents
- **Errors**: Increased errors
- **Turnover**: Increased staff turnover

**Workplace Safety:**
- **Accidents**: Drug-related workplace accidents
- **Injuries**: Increased workplace injuries
- **Fatalities**: Fatal workplace accidents
- **Safety-Sensitive Positions**: Particular risks in safety-sensitive positions
- **Public Safety**: Risks to public safety
- **Liability**: Employer liability for drug-related incidents

**Economic Impact:**
- **Lost Productivity**: Economic losses from reduced productivity
- **Healthcare Costs**: Increased healthcare costs
- **Workers' Compensation**: Increased workers' compensation claims
- **Training Costs**: Costs of training replacement workers
- **Insurance Costs**: Increased insurance premiums
- **Legal Costs**: Legal costs from drug-related issues

**Workplace Policies:**
- **Drug Testing**: Workplace drug testing policies
- **Drug-Free Workplace**: Drug-free workplace policies
- **Employee Assistance Programs**: EAPs for drug issues
- **Treatment Referrals**: Referrals to treatment programs
- **Return to Work**: Return-to-work programs after treatment
- **Accommodation**: Accommodation for prescription drug use

**Employer Challenges:**
- **Privacy**: Balancing privacy with safety
- **Legal Compliance**: Legal compliance with drug testing
- **Stigma**: Stigma affecting treatment seeking
- **Cost**: Cost of drug testing and programs
- **Effectiveness**: Effectiveness of workplace policies
- **Employee Relations**: Impact on employee relations

**The Workplace Impact Reality:**
- **Significant**: Significant economic and safety impact
- **Preventable**: Many impacts preventable through intervention
- **Balancing Act**: Balancing privacy with safety
- **Cost-Effective**: Prevention and treatment cost-effective
- **Policy Variation**: Variation in workplace policies
- **Recovery Possible**: Employees can recover and return to work
