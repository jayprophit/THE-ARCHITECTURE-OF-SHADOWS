# Chapter 220: Public Health Response

## Public Health Approaches to Drug-Related Health Issues

Public health approaches address drug use as a health issue:

**Public Health Framework:**
- **Health Focus**: Focus on health rather than criminal justice
- **Population Health**: Population-level approaches
- **Prevention**: Emphasis on prevention
- **Harm Reduction**: Harm reduction as core strategy
- **Treatment Access**: Emphasis on treatment access
- **Health Equity**: Health equity considerations

**Surveillance and Monitoring:**
- **Overdose Surveillance**: Overdose death surveillance
- **Drug Use Surveillance**: Monitoring drug use patterns
- **Infectious Disease Surveillance**: Monitoring infectious diseases
- **Treatment Data**: Monitoring treatment access and outcomes
- **Emerging Threats**: Monitoring emerging drug threats
- **Data-Driven Response**: Data-driven public health response

**Prevention Programs:**
- **Community Prevention**: Community-based prevention programs
- **School Prevention**: School-based prevention
- **Family Prevention**: Family-based prevention
- **Media Campaigns**: Public awareness campaigns
- **Environmental Strategies**: Environmental prevention strategies
- **Policy Interventions**: Policy interventions for prevention

**Harm Reduction Programs:**
- **Syringe Exchange**: Syringe exchange programs
- **Supervised Consumption Sites**: Supervised consumption sites
- **Naloxone Distribution**: Naloxone distribution programs
- **Drug Checking**: Drug checking services
- **Safe Injection Sites**: Safe injection sites
- **Outreach Programs**: Outreach to active drug users

**Treatment Expansion:**
- **Medication-Assisted Treatment**: Expansion of MAT
- **Treatment Access**: Improving treatment access
- **Insurance Coverage**: Insurance coverage for treatment
- **Workforce Development**: Developing treatment workforce
- **Quality Improvement**: Quality improvement in treatment
- **Integrated Care**: Integrated care for co-occurring disorders

**The Public Health Response Reality:**
- **Effective**: Public health approaches effective
- **Evidence-Based**: Evidence-based interventions
- **Cost-Effective**: Cost-effective approaches
- **Stigma Reduction**: Reduces stigma around drug use
- **Comprehensive**: Comprehensive approach to drug issues
- **Growing Acceptance**: Growing acceptance of public health approach
