# Chapter 124: Quality Assessment

## Evaluating Drug Quality

Quality assessment evaluates the value and safety of drugs:

**Quality Indicators:**
- **Purity**: Percentage of active substance
- **Appearance**: Visual appearance of product
- **Smell**: Odour characteristics
- **Taste**: Taste characteristics (risky method)
- **Texture**: Physical texture
- **Packaging**: Quality of packaging

**Visual Assessment:**
- **Colour**: Colour of substance
- **Crystal Structure**: Crystal structure for crystalline substances
- **Consistency**: Consistency of powder or solid
- **Moisture**: Moisture content
- **Foreign Matter**: Visible foreign matter
- **Particle Size**: Particle size distribution

**Subjective Assessment:**
- **Reputation**: Reputation of supplier
- **Price**: Price as quality indicator
- **Effects**: Effects as quality indicator
- **Consistency**: Consistency between batches
- **Peer Reports**: Reports from other users
- **Online Reviews**: Online reviews and reports

**Objective Assessment:**
- **Laboratory Testing**: Laboratory analysis for purity and composition
- **Reagent Testing**: Reagent testing for substance identification
- **Spectroscopy**: Spectroscopic analysis
- **Chromatography**: Chromatographic analysis
- **Mass Spectrometry**: Mass spectrometric analysis
- **Drug Checking**: Professional drug checking services

**Quality Variability:**
- **Batch Variation**: Variation between batches
- **Geographic Variation**: Variation by geographic region
- **Supplier Variation**: Variation between suppliers
- **Seasonal Variation**: Seasonal variation in quality
- **Market Conditions**: Market conditions affecting quality
- **Enforcement Pressure**: Enforcement pressure affecting quality

**The Quality Assessment Reality:**
- **Variable**: Highly variable quality in illicit market
- **Risk**: Quality variability creates health risks
- **Limited Information**: Limited information for consumers
- **Subjective**: Much assessment is subjective
- **Harm Reduction**: Harm reduction emphasises objective testing
- **Consumer Risk**: Consumers bear the risk
