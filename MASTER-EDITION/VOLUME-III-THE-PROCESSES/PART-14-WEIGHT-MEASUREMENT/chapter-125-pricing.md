# Chapter 125: Pricing

## Drug Market Economics

Drug pricing follows complex market dynamics:

**Pricing Factors:**
- **Purity**: Higher purity commands higher prices
- **Scarcity**: Scarcity increases prices
- **Location**: Geographic variation in prices
- **Risk**: Risk level affects pricing
- **Competition**: Competition affects prices
- **Market Conditions**: General market conditions

**Price Levels:**
- **Production Level**: Lowest price at production
- **Wholesale Level**: Higher price at wholesale
- **Distribution Level**: Higher price at distribution
- **Street Level**: Highest price at street level
- **Geographic Variation**: Significant geographic variation
- **Time Variation**: Prices fluctuate over time

**Price Markups:**
- **Production to Wholesale**: Significant markup
- **Wholesale to Retail**: Significant markup
- **Retail to Street**: Significant markup
- **Cumulative Markups**: Cumulative markups across supply chain
- **Risk Premium**: Risk premium built into prices
- **Profit Margins**: Profit margins at each level

**Substance Pricing:**
- **Cannabis**: Generally lower prices
- **Cocaine**: Higher prices, varies by purity
- **Heroin**: High prices, varies by purity
- **MDMA**: Moderate to high prices
- **LSD**: High potency, low price per dose
- **Fentanyl**: Extremely potent, low price per dose

**Price Fluctuations:**
- **Supply Shocks**: Supply shocks increase prices
- **Law Enforcement**: Enforcement pressure increases prices
- **Seasonal**: Seasonal price variations
- **Geographic**: Geographic price variations
- **Quality**: Quality variations affect prices
- **Competition**: Competition affects prices

**The Pricing Reality:**
- **Complex**: Complex pricing dynamics
- **Variable**: Highly variable prices
- **Risk-Based**: Risk-based pricing
- **Geographic**: Significant geographic variation
- **Market-Driven**: Market-driven pricing
- **Inflation**: Drug prices subject to inflation
