# Chapter 126: Legal Definitions of Weight

## How the Law Defines Drug Quantities

Legal definitions of drug weight determine charges and sentences:

**Legal Weight Definitions:**
- **Actual Weight**: Total weight of substance including cutting agents
- **Pure Weight**: Weight of pure active substance only
- **Mixture Weight**: Weight of drug mixture
- **Adulterant Weight**: Weight of adulterants included in total
- **Container Weight**: Sometimes includes packaging weight
- **Jurisdictional Variation**: Varies by jurisdiction

**Charging Thresholds:**
- **Possession**: Small amounts for personal possession
- **Possession with Intent**: Larger amounts indicating intent to distribute
- **Trafficking**: Large amounts triggering trafficking charges
- **Manufacturing**: Any amount involved in manufacturing
- **Distribution**: Any amount involved in distribution
- **Importation/Exportation**: Any amount involved in cross-border trade

**Sentencing Enhancements:**
- **Weight-Based Sentencing**: Sentences based on drug weight
- **Mandatory Minimums**: Mandatory minimum sentences for certain weights
- **Enhanced Penalties**: Enhanced penalties for large quantities
- **Aggravating Factors**: Weight as aggravating factor in sentencing
- **Federal vs. State**: Different thresholds for federal vs. state charges
- **International Variation**: International variation in thresholds

**Measurement Issues:**
- **Total Weight**: Legal system typically uses total weight
- **Pure Weight**: Rarely used in charging
- **Mixture Weight**: Includes cutting agents in total
- **Container Weight**: Sometimes includes packaging
- **Moisture Content**: Moisture included in total weight
- **Laboratory Analysis**: Laboratory analysis determines weight

**Legal Consequences:**
- **Possession Charges**: Charges based on weight
- **Distribution Charges**: Enhanced charges based on weight
- **Trafficking Charges**: Trafficking charges based on weight
- **Sentencing**: Sentencing based on weight thresholds
- **Asset Forfeiture**: Asset forfeiture based on weight
- **Enhanced Sentences**: Enhanced sentences for large quantities

**The Legal Weight Reality:**
- **Total Weight**: Legal system uses total weight including cutting agents
- **Harsh Consequences**: Harsh consequences for large quantities
- **Jurisdictional Variation**: Significant variation by jurisdiction
- **Mandatory Minimums**: Mandatory minimum sentences based on weight
- **Unfair**: Often unfair as cutting agents inflate weight
- **Reform**: Reform efforts to use pure weight instead
