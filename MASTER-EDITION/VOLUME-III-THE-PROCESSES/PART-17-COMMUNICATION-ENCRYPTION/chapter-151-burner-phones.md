# Chapter 151: Burner Phones

## Disposable Mobile Communication

Burner phones provide temporary, anonymous mobile communication:

**Burner Phone Characteristics:**
- **Prepaid**: Prepaid mobile phones
- **No Contract**: No contract required
- **Anonymous**: Can be purchased anonymously with cash
- **Disposable**: Disposable after use
- **Unregistered**: Often unregistered to real identity
- **Inexpensive**: Inexpensive to purchase

**Purchase Methods:**
- **Cash Purchase**: Cash purchase to avoid identification
- **Multiple Phones**: Purchase of multiple phones
- **No ID**: No ID required for prepaid phones in some jurisdictions
- **Online Purchase**: Online purchase with delivery to drop locations
- **Third-Party Purchase**: Third-party purchase to avoid identification
- **Bulk Purchase**: Bulk purchase for multiple operations

**Operational Use:**
- **Temporary Use**: Used for specific operations or time periods
- **Regular Rotation**: Regular rotation of phones
- **One-Time Use**: One-time use for specific communications
- **Geographic Separation**: Phones used in specific geographic areas
- **Role Separation**: Different phones for different roles
- **Disposal**: Disposal after use

**Security Practices:**
- **No Personal Info**: No personal information linked to phone
- **Separate Devices**: Separate from personal devices
- **Limited Use**: Limited use to minimise tracking
- **Regular Replacement**: Regular replacement of phones
- **Location Awareness**: Awareness of location tracking
- **Data Wiping**: Wiping data before disposal

**Law Enforcement Tracking:**
- **Cell Tower Tracking**: Cell tower tracking of phone location
- **Metadata Analysis**: Metadata analysis of communications
- **Device Identification**: Device identification through IMEI
- **Call Detail Records**: Call detail records from providers
- **Location Data**: Location data from phone
- **Surveillance**: Surveillance of phone use

**The Burner Phone Reality:**
- **Common**: Common in drug trade
- **Limited Anonymity**: Limited anonymity due to tracking
- **Temporary**: Temporary solution for communication
- **Risk**: Risk of tracking and identification
- **Adaptive**: Adaptive to surveillance methods
- **Cat and Mouse**: Constant cat and mouse with law enforcement
