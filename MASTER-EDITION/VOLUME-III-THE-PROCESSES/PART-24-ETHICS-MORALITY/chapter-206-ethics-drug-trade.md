# Chapter 206: Ethics and the Drug Trade

## Ethical Considerations in Drug Policy

Ethical considerations are central to debates about drug policy:

**Ethical Frameworks:**
- **Utilitarian Ethics**: Utilitarian approach to drug policy
- **Deontological Ethics**: Deontological approach to drug policy
- **Virtue Ethics**: Virtue ethics approach to drug policy
- **Public Health Ethics**: Public health ethics framework
- **Human Rights**: Human rights framework
- **Social Justice**: Social justice considerations

**Prohibition Ethics:**
- **Paternalism**: Paternalistic justification for prohibition
- **Harm Prevention**: Preventing harm to individuals and society
- **Moral Arguments**: Moral arguments against drug use
- **Protection of Vulnerable**: Protection of vulnerable populations
- **Social Order**: Maintenance of social order
- **Criticisms**: Ethical criticisms of prohibition

**Legalisation Ethics:**
- **Autonomy**: Respect for individual autonomy
- **Liberty**: Liberty arguments for legalisation
- **Harm Reduction**: Reducing harm through regulation
- **Social Justice**: Social justice arguments for reform
- **Racial Justice**: Racial justice considerations
- **Criticisms**: Ethical criticisms of legalisation

**Medical Ethics:**
- **Beneficence**: Beneficence in treatment
- **Non-Maleficence**: Do no harm principle
- **Autonomy**: Patient autonomy in treatment
- **Justice**: Justice in access to treatment
- **Informed Consent**: Informed consent in treatment
- **Research Ethics**: Ethics of drug research

**Research Ethics:**
- **Informed Consent**: Informed consent in drug research
- **Risk-Benefit Analysis**: Risk-benefit analysis in research
- **Vulnerable Populations**: Protection of vulnerable populations
- **Scientific Integrity**: Scientific integrity in research
- **Conflict of Interest**: Conflict of interest in research
- **Publication Ethics**: Ethics in publishing research

**The Ethics Reality:**
- **Complex**: Complex ethical considerations
- **Contested**: Contested ethical frameworks
- **Balancing**: Balancing competing ethical principles
- **Context-Dependent**: Ethical considerations depend on context
- **Evolving**: Evolving ethical understanding
- **Essential**: Essential for policy debates
