# Chapter 138: Dark Web Markets

## Online Drug Marketplaces

Dark web markets are online marketplaces for illicit drugs:

**Characteristics of Dark Web Markets:**
- **Anonymous**: Anonymous transactions through cryptocurrency
- **Global**: Global reach
- **User Reviews**: User reviews and ratings
- **Escrow Services**: Escrow services for buyer protection
- **Vendor Verification**: Vendor verification systems
- **Dispute Resolution**: Dispute resolution mechanisms

**Major Markets (Historical and Current):**
- **Silk Road**: First major dark web market (2011-2013)
- **Silk Road 2.0**: Successor to Silk Road (2013-2014)
- **AlphaBay**: Major market (2014-2017)
- **Hansa**: Major market (2015-2017)
- **Dream Market**: Long-running market (2013-2019)
- **Current Markets**: Various current markets (names change frequently)

**Market Features:**
- **Product Listings**: Detailed product listings with photos
- **Vendor Profiles**: Vendor profiles with ratings
- **User Reviews**: User reviews of vendors and products
- **Escrow**: Escrow holding funds until delivery confirmed
- **Dispute Resolution**: Dispute resolution for failed transactions
- **Forum**: Community forums for discussion

**Security Measures:**
- **Cryptocurrency**: Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies for payments
- **PGP Encryption**: PGP encryption for communications
- **2FA**: Two-factor authentication
- **Multi-Signature**: Multi-signature transactions
- **Vendor Bond**: Vendor bonds to ensure reliability
- **Security Practices**: Various security practices

**Law Enforcement Actions:**
- **Market Takedowns**: Law enforcement takedowns of major markets
- **Arrests**: Arrests of market operators and major vendors
- **Seizures**: Seizure of cryptocurrency and assets
- **Undercover Operations**: Undercover operations on markets
- **Exit Scams**: Some markets exit scamming users
- **Resilience**: Markets prove resilient to takedowns

**The Dark Web Market Reality:**
- **Resilient**: Resilient to law enforcement takedowns
- **Global**: Global drug distribution
- **Professional**: Professional operation of markets
- **Innovative**: Constantly innovating security
- **Challenging**: Challenging for law enforcement
- **Significant Impact**: Significant impact on drug trade
