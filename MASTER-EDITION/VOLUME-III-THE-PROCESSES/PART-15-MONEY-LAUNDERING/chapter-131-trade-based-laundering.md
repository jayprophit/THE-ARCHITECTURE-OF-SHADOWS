# Chapter 131: Trade-Based Money Laundering

## Using International Trade to Launder Money

Trade-based money laundering uses international trade to move money:

**Methods:**
- **Over-Invoicing**: Invoicing above actual value to move money
- **Under-Invoicing**: Invoicing below actual value to move money
- **False Invoicing**: False invoices for non-existent goods
- **Multiple Invoicing**: Multiple invoices for same goods
- **Phantom Shipments**: Phantom shipments that don't exist
- **Transfer Pricing**: Manipulating transfer pricing

**Over-Invoicing:**
- **Import Over-Invoicing**: Over-invoicing imports to move money out
- **Export Over-Invoicing**: Over-invoicing exports to move money in
- **Value Inflation**: Inflating value of goods
- **Quality Misrepresentation**: Misrepresenting quality to justify price
- **Quantity Misrepresentation**: Misrepresenting quantity
- **Documentation**: False documentation to support inflated prices

**Under-Invoicing:**
- **Import Under-Invoicing**: Under-invoicing imports to move money in
- **Export Under-Invoicing**: Under-invoicing exports to move money out
- **Value Deflation**: Deflating value of goods
- **Quality Misrepresentation**: Misrepresenting quality to justify low price
- **Quantity Misrepresentation**: Misrepresenting quantity
- **Documentation**: False documentation to support deflated prices

**False Invoicing:**
- **Non-Existent Goods**: Invoicing for non-existent goods
- **Double Invoicing**: Invoicing same goods multiple times
- **Shell Companies**: Shell companies issuing false invoices
- **Documentation**: False documentation for non-existent shipments
- **Payment**: Payment for non-existent goods
- **Integration**: Integration of funds through trade

**The Trade-Based Reality:**
- **Sophisticated**: Sophisticated money laundering method
- **International**: International scope complicates detection
- **Volume**: High volume of legitimate trade hides illicit activity
- **Challenging**: Extremely challenging to detect
- **Professional**: Professional expertise required
- **Growing**: Growing method of money laundering
