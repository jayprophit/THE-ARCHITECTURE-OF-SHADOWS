# Chapter 127: Money Laundering Overview

## The Process of Cleaning Dirty Money

Money laundering disguises illicit drug money as legitimate:

**The Three Stages:**
- **Placement**: Introducing illicit cash into financial system
- **Layering**: Moving money through multiple transactions to obscure origin
- **Integration**: Reintroducing money into legitimate economy as apparently legal funds

**Placement Stage:**
- **Cash Deposits**: Depositing cash in banks
- **Cash Businesses**: Using cash-intensive businesses to deposit money
- **Smurfing**: Breaking large cash amounts into small deposits
- **Currency Smuggling**: Physically moving cash across borders
- **Money Mules**: Using individuals to move money
- **Purchase of Assets**: Purchasing assets with cash

**Layering Stage:**
- **Wire Transfers**: Multiple wire transfers between accounts
- **Shell Companies**: Using shell companies to move money
- **Offshore Accounts**: Moving money to offshore jurisdictions
- **Investments**: Investing in legitimate businesses
- **Complex Transactions**: Complex financial transactions
- **International Movement**: Moving money across international borders

**Integration Stage:**
- **Legitimate Business**: Investing in legitimate businesses
- **Real Estate**: Purchasing real estate
- **Luxury Goods**: Purchasing luxury goods
- **False Invoices**: Using false invoices to move money
- **Loan Repayment**: Repaying loans with illicit money
- **Dividends**: Receiving dividends from legitimate investments

**Scale of Money Laundering:**
- **Global Scale**: Trillions of dollars laundered annually
- **Drug Trade**: Drug trade is major source of laundered money
- **Financial System**: Financial system essential for money laundering
- **Global Problem**: Global problem requiring international cooperation
- **Economic Impact**: Significant economic impact
- **Security Impact**: Security impact from money laundering

**The Money Laundering Reality:**
- **Essential**: Essential for drug trade
- **Sophisticated**: Increasingly sophisticated methods
- **Global**: Global operation
- **Financial System**: Dependent on financial system
- **Challenging**: Challenging to detect and prevent
- **Systemic**: Systemic problem requiring systemic solutions
