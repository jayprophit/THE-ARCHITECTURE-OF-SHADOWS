# Chapter 130: Integration

## Reintroducing Money into the Legitimate Economy

Integration is the final stage of money laundering:

**Integration Methods:**
- **Legitimate Business**: Investing in legitimate businesses
- **Real Estate**: Purchasing real estate
- **Luxury Goods**: Purchasing luxury goods
- **False Invoices**: Using false invoices to move money
- **Loan Repayment**: Repaying loans with illicit money
- **Dividends**: Receiving dividends from legitimate investments

**Business Integration:**
- **Business Acquisition**: Acquiring legitimate businesses
- **Business Investment**: Investing in legitimate businesses
- **Profit Generation**: Generating apparent legitimate profits
- **Business Operations**: Operating legitimate businesses
- **Employment**: Creating apparent legitimate employment
- **Tax Filing**: Filing tax returns on apparent legitimate income

**Real Estate Integration:**
- **Property Purchase**: Purchasing real estate with laundered money
- **Property Development**: Property development projects
- **Rental Income**: Generating rental income
- **Property Flipping**: Buying and quickly selling property
- **Commercial Property**: Commercial real estate investments
- **International Property**: International real estate investments

**Asset Integration:**
- **Luxury Goods**: Purchasing luxury goods (cars, watches, jewelry)
- **Art and Antiques**: Purchasing art and antiques
- **Precious Metals**: Purchasing gold and other precious metals
- **Yachts and Aircraft**: Purchasing yachts and aircraft
- **Collectibles**: Purchasing collectibles
- **High-Value Items**: Purchasing high-value items

**The Integration Reality:**
- **Final Stage**: Final stage of money laundering
- **Legitimacy**: Creates appearance of legitimate wealth
- **Long-Term**: Long-term integration into legitimate economy
- **Complex**: Complex integration methods
- **Professional**: Professional expertise required
- **Detection Challenge**: Challenging to detect once integrated
