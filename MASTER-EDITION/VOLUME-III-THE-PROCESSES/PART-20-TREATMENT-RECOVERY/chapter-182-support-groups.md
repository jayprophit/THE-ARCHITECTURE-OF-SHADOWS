# Chapter 182: Support Groups

## Peer Support in Recovery

Support groups provide peer support for recovery:

**12-Step Programs:**
- **Alcoholics Anonymous (AA)**: 12-step program for alcohol addiction
- **Narcotics Anonymous (NA)**: 12-step program for drug addiction
- **Cocaine Anonymous**: 12-step program for cocaine addiction
- **Crystal Meth Anonymous**: 12-step program for methamphetamine addiction
- **Marijuana Anonymous**: 12-step program for marijuana addiction
- **Other 12-Step Programs**: Various other 12-step programs

**12-Step Principles:**
- **Admitting Powerlessness**: Admitting powerlessness over addiction
- **Higher Power**: Belief in a higher power
- **Moral Inventory**: Taking moral inventory
- **Amends**: Making amends for past wrongs
- **Sponsorship**: Sponsorship for new members
- **Meetings**: Regular meeting attendance

**Non-12-Step Programs:**
- **SMART Recovery**: Science-based recovery program
- **Refuge Recovery**: Buddhist-inspired recovery program
- **LifeRing Secular Recovery**: Secular recovery program
- **Women for Sobriety**: Recovery program for women
- **Moderation Management**: Moderation rather than abstinence
- **Other Alternatives**: Various other alternatives to 12-step

**Online Support:**
- **Online Forums**: Online recovery forums
- **Social Media Groups**: Social media recovery groups
- **Virtual Meetings**: Virtual 12-step meetings
- **Apps**: Recovery support apps
- **Telehealth**: Telehealth support services
- **Online Communities**: Online recovery communities

**Benefits of Support Groups:**
- **Peer Support**: Peer support from others in recovery
- **Accountability**: Accountability to group
- **Social Connection**: Social connection reducing isolation
- **Shared Experience**: Shared experience and understanding
- **Cost-Effective**: Generally free or low-cost
- **Accessible**: Widely available

**The Support Group Reality:**
- **Widely Used**: Widely used in recovery
- **Effective**: Effective for many people
- **Varied Approaches**: Variety of approaches available
- **Peer Support**: Valuable peer support
- **Not for Everyone**: Not effective for everyone
- **Combination**: Often combined with other treatment
