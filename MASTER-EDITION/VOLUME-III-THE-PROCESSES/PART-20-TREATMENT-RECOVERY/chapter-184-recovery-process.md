# Chapter 184: The Recovery Process

## The Journey from Addiction to Recovery

Recovery is a process, not an event:

**Stages of Recovery:**
- **Pre-Contemplation**: Not yet considering change
- **Contemplation**: Considering change but not committed
- **Preparation**: Preparing to make changes
- **Action**: Taking active steps to change
- **Maintenance**: Maintaining change and preventing relapse
- **Termination**: Stable recovery with no risk of relapse

**Early Recovery:**
- **Detoxification**: Initial detoxification from substances
- **Withdrawal**: Managing withdrawal symptoms
- **Stabilisation**: Stabilising physically and emotionally
- **Treatment Entry**: Entering treatment programs
- **Crisis Management**: Managing crises in early recovery
- **Building Support**: Building support systems

**Mid-Recovery:**
- **Relapse Prevention**: Developing relapse prevention skills
- **Lifestyle Changes**: Making lifestyle changes
- **Relationship Repair**: Repairing damaged relationships
- **Skill Building**: Building skills for sober living
- **Identity Change**: Developing new identity in recovery
- **Community Integration**: Integrating into recovery community

**Long-Term Recovery:**
- **Maintenance**: Maintaining recovery over time
- **Growth**: Personal growth in recovery
- **Giving Back**: Helping others in recovery
- **Life Management**: Managing life without substances
- **Ongoing Support**: Ongoing support and connection
- **Recovery Capital**: Building recovery capital

**Relapse:**
- **Common**: Relapse is common in recovery
- **Learning Opportunity**: Opportunity to learn and strengthen recovery
- **Not Failure**: Relapse is not failure but part of process
- **Return to Treatment**: Return to treatment after relapse
- **Adjustment**: Adjustment of recovery plan after relapse
- **Resilience**: Building resilience through overcoming relapse

**The Recovery Reality:**
- **Process**: Recovery is a process, not an event
- **Non-Linear**: Non-linear journey with setbacks
- **Individual**: Individual recovery paths
- **Possible**: Recovery is possible
- **Ongoing**: Ongoing process of growth
- **Hope**: Hope for lasting recovery
