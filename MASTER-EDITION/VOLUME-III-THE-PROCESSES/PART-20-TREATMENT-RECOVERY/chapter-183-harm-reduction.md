# Chapter 183: Harm Reduction

## Reducing Drug-Related Harm

Harm reduction aims to reduce the negative consequences of drug use:

**Harm Reduction Principles:**
- **Non-Judgmental**: Non-judgmental approach to drug use
- **User-Centred**: Centred on the needs of drug users
- **Pragmatic**: Pragmatic approach focused on reducing harm
- **Evidence-Based**: Evidence-based interventions
- **Human Rights**: Respect for human rights
- **Access**: Access to services without barriers

**Syringe Exchange Programs:**
- **Clean Syringes**: Providing clean syringes to prevent disease transmission
- **Needle Exchange**: Exchange of used syringes for clean ones
- **HIV Prevention**: Prevention of HIV transmission
- **Hepatitis Prevention**: Prevention of hepatitis transmission
- **Safe Disposal**: Safe disposal of used syringes
- **Access to Services**: Access to other health services

**Supervised Consumption Sites:**
- **Safe Consumption**: Safe spaces for drug consumption
- **Medical Supervision**: Medical supervision during consumption
- **Overdose Prevention**: Overdose prevention and response
- **Health Services**: Access to health services
- **Referral to Treatment**: Referral to treatment services
- **Community Benefits**: Community benefits from reduced public drug use

**Naloxone Distribution:**
- **Overdose Reversal**: Naloxone for opioid overdose reversal
- **Take-Home Naloxone**: Take-home naloxone for drug users and families
- **Training**: Training on overdose recognition and response
- **Pharmacy Access**: Pharmacy access to naloxone
- **First Responder Carry**: First responders carrying naloxone
- **Lives Saved**: Lives saved through naloxone distribution

**Drug Checking Services:**
- **Substance Testing**: Testing drug substances for purity and adulterants
- **Harm Information**: Providing information about drug risks
- **Fentanyl Detection**: Detection of fentanyl contamination
- **Festival Services**: Drug checking at festivals and events
- **Laboratory Analysis**: Laboratory analysis of substances
- **Consumer Protection**: Protecting consumers from dangerous substances

**The Harm Reduction Reality:**
- **Effective**: Effective at reducing harm
- **Controversial**: Controversial despite evidence
- **Evidence-Based**: Strong evidence base
- **Life-Saving**: Life-saving interventions
- **Underfunded**: Often underfunded
- **Expanding**: Expanding acceptance and implementation
