# Chapter 195: Alcohol and Tobacco Regulation

## Regulating Legal Psychoactive Substances

Alcohol and tobacco are legal but regulated psychoactive substances:

**Alcohol Regulation:**
- **Minimum Legal Drinking Age**: Minimum age for alcohol purchase
- **Licensing**: Licensing of alcohol sales establishments
- **Hours of Sale**: Restrictions on hours of sale
- **Advertising Restrictions**: Restrictions on alcohol advertising
- **Taxation**: Alcohol taxation
- **Driving Under Influence**: DUI laws and enforcement

**Tobacco Regulation:**
- **Minimum Legal Age**: Minimum age for tobacco purchase
- **Smoke-Free Laws**: Smoke-free laws for public places
- **Advertising Bans**: Bans on tobacco advertising
- **Plain Packaging**: Plain packaging requirements
- **Taxation**: Tobacco taxation
- **Health Warnings**: Health warning labels

**Regulatory Approaches:**
- **Supply Restrictions**: Restrictions on supply
- **Demand Reduction**: Efforts to reduce demand
- **Harm Reduction**: Harm reduction approaches
- **Public Health**: Public health focus
- **Industry Regulation**: Regulation of industry practices
- **Consumer Protection**: Consumer protection measures

**Effectiveness:**
- **Price Effects**: Price effects on consumption
- **Advertising Effects**: Advertising effects on consumption
- **Age Restrictions**: Effectiveness of age restrictions
- **Smoke-Free Laws**: Effectiveness of smoke-free laws
- **Plain Packaging**: Effectiveness of plain packaging
- **Taxation**: Effectiveness of taxation

**The Alcohol Tobacco Regulation Reality:**
- **Legal but Regulated**: Legal but regulated substances
- **Public Health Focus**: Public health focus of regulation
- **Effective**: Many regulations effective
- **Industry Influence**: Industry influence on regulation
- **Evolving**: Evolving regulatory approaches
- **Evidence-Based**: Increasingly evidence-based regulation
