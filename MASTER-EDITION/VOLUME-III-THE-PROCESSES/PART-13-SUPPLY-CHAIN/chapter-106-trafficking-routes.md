# Chapter 106: The Trafficking Routes

## Global Drug Transportation Networks

Drug trafficking routes span the globe:

**Major Cocaine Routes:**
- **South America to North America**: Through Central America and Mexico
- **South America to Europe**: Through West Africa and the Caribbean
- **South America to Asia**: Emerging routes to Asian markets
- **South America to Africa**: Growing African market
- **Intra-Regional Routes**: Within South America

**Major Heroin Routes:**
- **Golden Triangle to China**: Through Southeast Asia
- **Golden Crescent to Europe**: Through Balkans and Turkey
- **Golden Crescent to Russia**: Through Central Asia
- **Afghanistan to India**: Through South Asia
- **Myanmar to Bangladesh**: Through Southeast Asia

**Major Methamphetamine Routes:**
- **Southeast Asia to East Asia**: Regional distribution
- **China to Southeast Asia**: Regional distribution
- **Mexico to North America**: North American distribution
- **Iran to Middle East**: Regional distribution
- **Global Distribution**: Increasing global distribution

**Major Cannabis Routes:**
- **Morocco to Europe**: Through Spain
- **Afghanistan to Central Asia**: Regional distribution
- **Mexico to North America**: North American distribution
- **Domestic Production**: Increasing domestic production globally
- **Global Distribution**: Global distribution of various cannabis products

**Route Characteristics:**
- **Multi-Modal**: Combination of air, sea, and land transport
- **Adaptive**: Constantly adapting to enforcement pressure
- **Historical**: Some routes have historical continuity
- **Emerging**: New routes constantly emerging
- **Seasonal**: Some routes are seasonal
- **Conflict Zones**: Routes often through conflict zones

**The Trafficking Route Reality:**
- **Global Network**: Global network of routes
- **Adaptive**: Constantly adapting to conditions
- **Complex**: Complex multi-modal networks
- **Vulnerable**: Vulnerable to interdiction
- **Resilient**: Resilient to disruption
- **Strategic**: Strategic importance to drug trade
