# Chapter 115: Drugs in Clubs, Events, and Holiday Destinations

## Nightlife and Tourism Drug Markets

Drug use is common in nightlife and tourism contexts:

**Clubs and Nightlife:**
- **Nightclubs**: High rates of drug use in nightclubs
- **Bars**: Drug use in bars and pubs
- **Late-Night Venues**: Drug use in late-night venues
- **Music Venues**: Drug use at music venues
- **Private Parties**: Drug use at private parties
- **Underground Venues**: Drug use in underground venues

**Events and Festivals:**
- **Raves**: Drug use at rave events
- **Electronic Music Events**: Drug use at electronic music events
- **Club Nights**: Drug use at club nights
- **Private Events**: Drug use at private events
- **Pop-Up Events**: Drug use at pop-up events
- **Underground Events**: Drug use at underground events

**Holiday Destinations:**
- **Party Destinations**: Drug use at party destinations (Ibiza, etc.)
- **Beach Resorts**: Drug use at beach resorts
- **Ski Resorts**: Drug use at ski resorts
- **Tourist Areas**: Drug use in tourist areas
- **Backpacker Destinations**: Drug use in backpacker destinations
- **Cruise Ships**: Drug use on cruise ships

**Common Substances:**
- **MDMA/Ecstasy**: Common in club and festival settings
- **Cocaine**: Common in nightlife settings
- **Ketamine**: Common in club settings
- **GHB**: Common in club settings
- **Cannabis**: Common across all settings
- **Alcohol**: Often combined with other substances

**Enforcement Challenges:**
- **High Volume**: High volume of venues and events
- **Temporary Nature**: Temporary nature of many events
- **Resource Constraints**: Limited resources for comprehensive enforcement
- **Public Safety**: Balancing enforcement with public safety
- **Tourism Impact**: Impact on tourism industry
- **Local Economy**: Economic impact on local economies

**Harm Reduction:**
- **Drug Checking**: Drug checking services in some venues
- **Medical Services**: Medical services at large events
- **Information**: Information about drug risks
- **Safe Spaces**: Safe spaces for distressed users
- **Naloxone**: Naloxone availability in some venues
- **Staff Training**: Staff training on drug incidents

**The Nightlife Drug Reality:**
- **Integrated**: Drug use integrated into nightlife culture
- **Economic**: Significant economic impact on nightlife industry
- **Safety Risk**: Safety risks for users
- **Enforcement Challenge**: Significant enforcement challenge
- **Harm Reduction**: Growing harm reduction approach
- **Cultural**: Deeply embedded in youth culture
