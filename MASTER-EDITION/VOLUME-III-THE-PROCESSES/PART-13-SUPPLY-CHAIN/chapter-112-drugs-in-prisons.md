# Chapter 112: Drugs in Prisons

## The Prison Drug Market

Drugs flow into prisons despite strict security:

**How Drugs Enter Prisons:**
- **Visitors**: Visitors bringing drugs in
- **Staff**: Corrupt staff bringing drugs in
- **Mail**: Drugs hidden in mail packages
- **Drones**: Drones dropping drugs into prison yards
- **Throw-Overs**: Drugs thrown over prison walls
- **Internal Production**: Sometimes drugs produced internally

**Prison Drug Markets:**
- **Higher Prices**: Significantly higher prices than outside
- **Lower Quality**: Often lower quality than outside
- **Violent Enforcement**: Violent enforcement of market control
- **Gang Control**: Gangs controlling prison drug markets
- **Debt**: Prisoners accumulating drug debts
- **Currency**: Drugs as currency in prison economy

**Prison Drug Use:**
- **Continued Use**: Continued drug use by addicts
- **New Use**: Some prisoners start using drugs in prison
- **Self-Medication**: Self-medication for mental health issues
- **Coping**: Coping mechanism for prison life
- **Social Pressure**: Social pressure to use drugs
- **Addiction**: Addiction continuing from outside

**Consequences:**
- **Violence**: Drug-related violence in prisons
- **Debt**: Prisoners accumulating drug debts
- **Overdoses**: Overdose deaths in prisons
- **Health Problems**: Health problems from drug use
- **Rehabilitation Failure**: Drug use undermines rehabilitation
- **Reoffending**: Drug use increases reoffending risk

**Prevention Efforts:**
- **Security**: Enhanced security measures
- **Searches**: Regular searches of prisoners and cells
- **Drug Testing**: Regular drug testing
- **Treatment**: Drug treatment programs in prisons
- **Harm Reduction**: Limited harm reduction measures
- **Staff Training**: Training staff to detect drugs

**The Prison Drug Reality:**
- **Persistent**: Persistent problem despite security
- **Expensive**: Extremely expensive prison drug market
- **Violent**: Violence associated with drug market
- **Complex**: Complex supply chains into prisons
- **Challenging**: Challenging to prevent
- **Systemic**: Systemic issue requiring systemic solutions
