# Chapter 111: The Logistics

## Supply Chain Management

Drug trade logistics mirror legitimate supply chains:

**Logistics Components:**
- **Transportation**: Moving drugs from production to consumption
- **Warehousing**: Temporary storage of drugs
- **Distribution**: Distributing to various markets
- **Inventory Management**: Managing drug inventory
- **Coordination**: Coordinating across multiple locations
- **Risk Management**: Managing risks of interdiction

**Transportation Logistics:**
- **Multi-Modal**: Air, sea, and land transportation
- **Route Planning**: Careful route planning to avoid detection
- **Timing**: Careful timing to avoid detection
- **Security**: Security of shipments
- **Backup Plans**: Backup plans for disrupted routes
- **Communication**: Coordinated communication

**Warehousing and Storage:**
- **Safe Houses**: Temporary storage locations
- **Distribution Centers**: Central distribution points
- **Security**: Security of storage locations
- **Inventory Control**: Control of inventory
- **Rotation**: Rotation of inventory
- **Disposal**: Disposal of compromised inventory

**Distribution Logistics:**
- **Wholesale Distribution**: Distribution to wholesalers
- **Retail Distribution**: Distribution to street dealers
- **Geographic Coverage**: Geographic coverage of distribution
- **Market Segmentation**: Different products for different markets
- **Pricing Strategy**: Pricing strategy across distribution levels
- **Customer Service**: Customer service for repeat business

**Risk Management:**
- **Interdiction Risk**: Managing risk of law enforcement interdiction
- **Theft Risk**: Managing risk of theft
- **Quality Risk**: Managing quality risks
- **Market Risk**: Managing market fluctuations
- **Legal Risk**: Managing legal risks
- **Violence Risk**: Managing violence risks

**The Logistics Reality:**
- **Sophisticated**: Increasingly sophisticated logistics
- **Professional**: Professional logistics operations
- **Adaptive**: Adapting to enforcement pressure
- **Efficient**: Efficient supply chain management
- **Global**: Global logistics networks
- **Vulnerable**: Vulnerable to disruption
