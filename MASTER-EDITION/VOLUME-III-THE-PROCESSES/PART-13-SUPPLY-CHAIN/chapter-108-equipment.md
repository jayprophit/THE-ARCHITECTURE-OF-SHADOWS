# Chapter 108: The Equipment

## Tools of the Drug Trade

The drug trade requires specialised equipment:

**Production Equipment:**
- **Laboratory Equipment**: Glassware, heating elements, reactors
- **Synthesis Equipment**: Equipment for chemical synthesis
- **Extraction Equipment**: Equipment for extracting active compounds
- **Purification Equipment**: Equipment for purifying compounds
- **Pill Presses**: Equipment for pressing pills
- **Packaging Equipment**: Equipment for packaging drugs

**Cultivation Equipment:**
- **Grow Lights**: LED, HPS, MH lights for indoor cultivation
- **Grow Tents**: Enclosed growing spaces
- **Ventilation**: Fans, filters, ducting
- **Hydroponic Systems**: Soil-less cultivation systems
- **Nutrient Systems**: Nutrient delivery systems
- **Environmental Control**: Temperature and humidity control

**Smuggling Equipment:**
- **Hidden Compartments**: Secret compartments in vehicles
- **Submersibles**: Submersible vehicles for maritime smuggling
- **Communication Equipment**: Encrypted communication devices
- **Counter-Surveillance**: Counter-surveillance equipment
- **Packaging**: Specialised packaging for smuggling
- **Navigation**: GPS and navigation equipment

**Distribution Equipment:**
- **Scales**: Precision scales for measuring drugs
- **Packaging**: Packaging materials for street-level distribution
- **Security**: Security equipment for storage
- **Transportation**: Vehicles for local distribution
- **Communication**: Communication equipment for coordination
- **Weapons**: Weapons for protection

**Money Laundering Equipment:**
- **Counterfeit Detection**: Equipment for detecting counterfeit currency
- **Money Counting**: Money counting machines
- **Cryptocurrency**: Equipment for cryptocurrency operations
- **Secure Storage**: Secure storage for cash
- **Banking**: Banking equipment and access
- **Legal**: Legal documents and corporate structures

**The Equipment Reality:**
- **Specialised**: Highly specialised equipment
- **Legal Sources**: Much equipment has legitimate sources
- **Dual Use**: Much equipment has dual use
- **Regulation**: Increasing regulation of equipment
- **Innovation**: Constant innovation in equipment
- **Vulnerability**: Equipment supply chain vulnerable to disruption
