# Chapter 323: Compartmentalization

## The Structure of Secrecy

Compartmentalization is the key to maintaining the shadow system:

**Need to Know:**
- **Access Control**: Strict access control based on need to know
- **Information Segmentation**: Segmentation of information
- **Clearance Levels**: Multiple clearance levels
- **Compartment Codes**: Compartment codes for access
- **Access Logs**: Detailed access logging
- **Security Clearances**: Security clearance systems

**Layers of Secrecy:**
- **Public Layer**: Public information layer
- **Classified Layer**: Classified information layer
- **Secret Layer**: Secret information layer
- **Top Secret Layer**: Top secret information layer
- **Special Access**: Special access programs
- **Black Programs**: Black programs beyond classification

**Compartment Structures:**
- **Vertical Compartmentalization**: Vertical compartmentalization by level
- **Horizontal Compartmentalization**: Horizontal compartmentalization by topic
- **Program Compartmentalization**: Program-based compartmentalization
- **Operational Compartmentalization**: Operational compartmentalization
- **Geographic Compartmentalization**: Geographic compartmentalization
- **Temporal Compartmentalization**: Temporal compartmentalization

**Information Control:**
- **Document Classification**: Document classification systems
- **Handling Procedures**: Special handling procedures
- **Communication Security**: Secure communication channels
- **Data Segregation**: Data segregation systems
- **Access Reviews**: Regular access reviews
- **Compromise Response**: Response to compromises

**The Compartmentalization Reality:**
- **Essential**: Essential to shadow system
- **Effective**: Highly effective at maintaining secrecy
- **Complex**: Extremely complex system
- **Vulnerable**: Vulnerable to leaks
- **Evolving**: Evolving with technology
- **Revealing**: Leaks revealing the system
