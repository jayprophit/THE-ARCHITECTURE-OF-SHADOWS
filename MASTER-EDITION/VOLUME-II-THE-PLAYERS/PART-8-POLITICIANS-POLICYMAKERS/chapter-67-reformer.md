# Chapter 67: The "Reformer"

## Advocating for Drug Policy Change

Reformers advocate for changes to drug policy:

**Roles and Responsibilities:**
- **Advocacy**: Advocating for drug policy reform
- **Research**: Conducting and presenting research on drug policy
- **Public Education**: Educating the public on drug policy issues
- **Policy Development**: Developing alternative policy proposals
- **Coalition Building**: Building coalitions for reform
- **Media Engagement**: Engaging with media on drug issues

**Reform Approaches:**
- **Decriminalisation**: Removing criminal penalties for personal use
- **Legalisation**: Creating legal regulated markets
- **Harm Reduction**: Emphasis on reducing harm rather than punishment
- **Treatment Focus**: Emphasis on treatment over incarceration
- **Human Rights**: Framing drug policy as human rights issue
- **Public Health**: Framing drug policy as public health issue

**Reform Organisations:**
- **Transform Drug Policy Foundation**: UK-based drug policy reform organisation
- **Drug Policy Alliance**: US-based drug policy reform organisation
- **Release**: UK drug information and advocacy organisation
- **International Drug Policy Consortium**: International network of drug policy organisations
- **European Drug Policy Initiative**: European drug policy reform network
- **Global Commission on Drug Policy**: High-level commission advocating reform

**Challenges:**
- **Political Resistance**: Political resistance to reform
- **Public Opinion**: Public opinion often opposes reform
- **Media Bias**: Media bias against reform positions
- **Resource Constraints**: Limited resources for advocacy
- **Stigma**: Stigma associated with drug policy reform
- **Implementation**: Challenges of implementing reform policies

**The Reformer's Perspective:**
- **Evidence-Based**: Commitment to evidence-based policy
- **Human Rights**: Commitment to human rights
- **Public Health**: Focus on public health approach
- **Social Justice**: Focus on social justice
- **Long-Term Vision**: Long-term vision for drug policy
