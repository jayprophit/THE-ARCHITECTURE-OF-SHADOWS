# Chapter 37: The Wholesaler

## The Middleman Between Cartels and Street Dealers

Wholesalers operate at the mid-level of the drug trade:

**Position in Supply Chain:**
- **Above Street Level**: Buy in bulk from higher-level suppliers
- **Below Cartels**: Not directly involved in international trafficking
- **Regional Distribution**: Supply multiple street dealers in a region
- **Volume Handling**: Handle significant quantities of drugs
- **Risk Management**: Balance profit with risk of detection

**Characteristics:**
- **Business Approach**: Often treat dealing as business
- **Discretion**: Maintain low profile, avoid direct customer contact
- **Network**: Have established network of street dealer customers
- **Capital**: Require significant capital for bulk purchases
- **Logistics**: Manage storage, transport, and distribution

## Logistics and Distribution

**Supply Chain Management:**
- **Bulk Purchases**: Buy kilograms or larger quantities
- **Storage**: Secure storage facilities
- **Transportation**: Move drugs between locations
- **Quality Control**: May test and repackage drugs
- **Distribution**: Supply network of street dealers

**Business Operations:**
- **Credit**: May extend credit to trusted dealers
- **Territories**: May control specific geographic areas
- **Competition**: Compete with other wholesalers
- **Relationships**: Maintain relationships with suppliers and customers
- **Profit Margins**: Lower margins than street dealers but higher volume

**Risk Management:**
- **Discretion**: Avoid drawing attention to operations
- **Encryption**: Use encrypted communications
- **Security**: Security measures for storage and transport
- **Legal Cover**: May have legitimate businesses as cover
- **Violence**: May use violence to protect territory

**Financial Aspects:**
- **Capital Intensive**: Require significant upfront capital
- **Cash Flow**: Manage significant cash flows
- **Money Laundering**: Need to launder large amounts of cash
- **Investment**: Reinvest profits in operations
- **Tax Evasion**: No taxes paid on illegal income

**Legal Risks:**
- **Higher Penalties**: Larger quantities mean harsher sentences
- **Police Attention**: Target of law enforcement investigations
- **Informants**: Vulnerable to informants in network
- **Surveillance**: Subject to surveillance operations
- **Asset Forfeiture**: Risk of losing assets and cash

**The Wholesaler's Role:**
- **Critical Link**: Essential link between international supply and street-level distribution
- **Efficiency**: Improve efficiency of drug distribution
- **Risk Distribution**: Distribute risk across network
- **Profit**: Significant profits from bulk operations
- **Vulnerability**: Critical vulnerability in supply chain for law enforcement
