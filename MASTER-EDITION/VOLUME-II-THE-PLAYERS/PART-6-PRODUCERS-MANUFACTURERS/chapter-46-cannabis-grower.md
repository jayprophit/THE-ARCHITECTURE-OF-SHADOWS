# Chapter 46: The Cannabis Grower

## Hobbyist to Industrial

Cannabis cultivation ranges from small-scale to industrial:

**Scale of Cultivation:**
- **Personal Grow**: Small-scale for personal use
- **Small Commercial**: Small-scale commercial operations
- **Large Commercial**: Industrial-scale cultivation
- **Legal Markets**: Licensed cultivation in legal jurisdictions
- **Illegal Markets**: Unlicensed cultivation in illegal contexts

**Cultivation Methods:**
- **Outdoor**: Natural sunlight, seasonal
- **Indoor**: Artificial lights, controlled environment
- **Greenhouse**: Combination of natural and artificial light
- **Hydroponics**: Soil-less cultivation
- **Aeroponics**: Mist-based cultivation
- **Organic**: Natural cultivation methods

## The Risks and Rewards

**Rewards:**
- **High Profits**: Cannabis can be very profitable
- **Growing Demand**: Increasing global demand
- **Legalisation**: Legal markets creating new opportunities
- **Skill Development**: Cultivation requires developed skills
- **Entrepreneurship**: Opportunity for small business owners

**Risks:**
- **Legal Risks**: Criminal penalties in illegal contexts
- **Violence**: Risk of robbery and violence
- **Fire Hazard**: Indoor grows pose fire risks
- **Mould and Pests**: Cultivation challenges
- **Market Fluctuations**: Prices can fluctuate significantly
- **Competition**: Increasing competition in legal markets

**Legal vs. Illegal Cultivation:**
- **Legal Markets**: Licensed, regulated, taxed
- **Illegal Markets**: Unlicensed, unregulated, untaxed
- **Quality Control**: Legal markets have quality standards
- **Testing**: Legal markets require testing for potency and contaminants
- **Economic Impact**: Legal markets create tax revenue and jobs

**The Cannabis Grower's Reality:**
- **Skill Intensive**: Requires horticultural knowledge
- **Capital Intensive**: Requires significant investment for commercial operations
- **Time Intensive**: Requires constant attention
- **Technical Knowledge**: Understanding of plant biology, lighting, nutrients
- **Economic Opportunity**: Significant economic opportunity in legal markets
