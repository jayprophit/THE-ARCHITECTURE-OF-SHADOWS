# Chapter 48: The Pill Presser

## Manufacturing Ecstasy and Counterfeit Pharmaceuticals

Pill pressers manufacture pressed tablets:

**Products:**
- **MDMA/Ecstasy**: Pressed MDMA tablets
- **Counterfeit Pharmaceuticals**: Fake prescription pills (Xanax, oxycodone)
- **Mixed Substances**: Pills containing multiple substances
- **Branded Pills**: Pills with logos or stamps
- **Variable Potency**: Potency varies significantly between batches

**Equipment:**
- **Pill Presses**: Industrial and small-scale presses
- **Dies and Punches**: Create pill shape and imprints
- **Mixing Equipment**: Blend active ingredients with fillers
- **Quality Control**: Variable quality control
- **Packaging**: Packaging equipment for final product

## Dies and Punches

**Tooling:**
- **Custom Dies**: Create specific shapes and imprints
- **Logos and Stamps**: Brand pills with logos or stamps
- **Counterfeit Appearance**: Make pills look like legitimate pharmaceuticals
- **Variety**: Wide variety of dies available
- **Acquisition**: Dies can be purchased or manufactured

**Counterfeiting:**
- **Pharmaceutical Counterfeits**: Fake versions of legitimate medications
- **Appearance**: Mimic appearance of real pills
- **Packaging**: Counterfeit packaging
- **Risk**: Users may not know they're taking counterfeits
- **Fentanyl Contamination**: Many counterfeit pills contain fentanyl

**The Pill Presser's Role:**
- **Critical Link**: Essential for pill-based drug market
- **Technical Skill**: Requires technical knowledge
- **Equipment Access**: Requires access to pill presses
- **Risk**: Risk of arrest and prosecution
- **Public Health Risk**: Counterfeit pills pose significant public health risk
