# Chapter 50: The Equipment Supplier

## The Enablers of Production

Equipment suppliers provide the tools for drug production:

**Products Supplied:**
- **Grow Equipment**: Lights, tents, fans, nutrients
- **Laboratory Equipment**: Glassware, scales, heating elements
- **Chemical Supplies**: Precursor chemicals, solvents
- **Pill Presses**: Equipment for pressing pills
- **Packaging**: Bags, containers, labels

**Types of Suppliers:**
- **Legitimate Businesses**: Legal businesses whose products are used for illegal purposes
- **Specialist Suppliers**: Businesses specifically serving the drug trade
- **Online Suppliers**: Internet-based suppliers
- **Physical Stores**: Retail stores (grow shops)
- **Wholesale Suppliers**: Bulk suppliers to other suppliers

## Grow Shops

**Grow Shop Products:**
- **Lighting**: LED, HPS, MH lights
- **Grow Tents**: Enclosed growing spaces
- **Ventilation**: Fans, filters, ducting
- **Nutrients**: Fertilisers and supplements
- **Growing Mediums**: Soil, coco coir, hydroponic systems
- **Cloning Equipment**: Propagators, cloning gels

**Legal Status:**
- **Legitimate Business**: Grow shops are legal businesses
- **Dual Use**: Products can be used for legal or illegal cultivation
- **Regulation**: Some jurisdictions regulate grow shop sales
- **Monitoring**: Some grow shops monitored by law enforcement
- **Knowledge**: Staff often knowledgeable about cultivation

## Laboratory and Chemical Supply Companies — Scientific Glassware, Chemical Precursors

**Laboratory Equipment:**
- **Glassware**: Flasks, beakers, condensers
- **Heating**: Hot plates, heating mantles
- **Measurement**: Scales, measuring equipment
- **Safety**: Safety equipment, fume hoods
- **Specialised Equipment**: Rotary evaporators, distillation equipment

**Chemical Supplies:**
- **Precursors**: Chemicals used in drug synthesis
- **Solvents**: Chemicals used in extraction and purification
- **Reagents**: Chemicals used in reactions
- **Regulation**: Many precursor chemicals are regulated
- **Diversion**: Diversion of legal chemicals for illegal use

**Legal Issues:**
- **Legitimate Use**: Most equipment and chemicals have legitimate uses
- **Regulation**: Increasing regulation of precursor chemicals
- **Monitoring**: Suppliers monitored for suspicious orders
- **Diversion Prevention**: Programs to prevent chemical diversion
- **International Cooperation**: International monitoring of chemical trade

**The Equipment Supplier's Role:**
- **Critical Enabler**: Essential for drug production
- **Legitimate Business**: Often legitimate businesses
- **Dual Use**: Products have legitimate and illegal uses
- **Regulatory Pressure**: Increasing regulation and monitoring
- **Knowledge**: Often knowledgeable about potential illegal uses
