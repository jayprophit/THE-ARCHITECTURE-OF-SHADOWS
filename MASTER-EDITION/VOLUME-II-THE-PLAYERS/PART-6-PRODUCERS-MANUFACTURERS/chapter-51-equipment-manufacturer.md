# Chapter 51: The Equipment Manufacturer

## The Legal Companies Supplying the Trade

Equipment manufacturers produce the tools used in drug production:

**Products Manufactured:**
- **Grow Equipment**: Lights, fans, environmental controllers
- **Laboratory Equipment**: Glassware, heating equipment, scales
- **Chemical Equipment**: Reactors, distillation equipment
- **Pill Presses**: Industrial and small-scale presses
- **Packaging Equipment**: Packaging machinery

**Major Suppliers:**
- **Hydrofarm**: Leading manufacturer of hydroponic equipment
- **GrowGeneration**: US-based specialty retailer and distributor
- **iPower**: Leading supplier of indoor growing systems
- **Alfa Aesar**: Supplier of precursor chemicals
- **BASF**: Leading supplier of specialty chemicals

## Legal and Ethical Considerations

**Legitimate Business:**
- **Legal Products**: Equipment has legitimate uses
- **Dual Use**: Products used for legal and illegal purposes
- **No Direct Knowledge**: Often no direct knowledge of illegal use
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Must comply with regulations
- **Corporate Responsibility**: Increasing focus on corporate responsibility

**Ethical Issues:**
- **Knowledge of Misuse**: May know products are used illegally
- **Profit from Illegal Trade**: Profiting indirectly from illegal trade
- **Responsibility**: Question of responsibility for downstream use
- **Due Diligence**: Increasing requirements for due diligence
- **Supply Chain Management**: Managing supply chain to prevent diversion

**Regulatory Pressure:**
- **Increased Regulation**: Increasing regulation of equipment sales
- **Monitoring**: Monitoring of suspicious orders
- **Reporting Requirements**: Requirements to report suspicious orders
- **International Cooperation**: International monitoring of equipment trade
- **Industry Self-Regulation**: Some industries implementing self-regulation

**The Equipment Manufacturer's Role:**
- **Critical Enabler**: Essential for drug production
- **Legitimate Business**: Legitimate companies with legitimate products
- **Dual Use Dilemma**: Products used for legal and illegal purposes
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Increasing regulatory requirements
- **Corporate Responsibility**: Growing focus on preventing misuse
