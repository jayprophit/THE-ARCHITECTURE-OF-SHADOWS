# Chapter 72: The Real Estate Agent

## Property-Based Money Laundering

Real estate agents facilitate money laundering through property:

**Roles in Money Laundering:**
- **Property Purchases**: Facilitating property purchases with drug money
- **Cash Transactions**: Handling cash transactions for property
- **Shell Companies**: Using shell companies to purchase property
- **Offshore Buyers**: Facilitating offshore buyers purchasing property
- **Undervaluation**: Purchasing property below market value
- **Rapid Flipping**: Buying and quickly selling property to launder money

**Money Laundering Methods:**
- **Cash Purchases**: Using cash to purchase property
- **Shell Companies**: Using shell companies to hide ownership
- **Offshore Structures**: Using offshore structures to purchase property
- **Overvaluation**: Paying above market value to move money
- **Rapid Resale**: Quick resale to "clean" money
- **Rental Income**: Using rental income to legitimise drug money

**Red Flags:**
- **Cash Purchases**: Large cash purchases of property
- **Shell Companies**: Purchases by shell companies
- **Offshore Buyers**: Purchases by offshore entities
- **Unusual Transactions**: Unusual transaction patterns
- **Undervaluation**: Purchases below market value
- **Rapid Flipping**: Rapid buying and selling

**The Real Estate Agent's Perspective:**
- **Commission**: Commission-driven motivation
- **Market Knowledge**: Knowledge of local property market
- **Client Relationships**: Relationships with clients
- **Ethical Dilemmas**: Ethical dilemmas about suspicious transactions
- **Legal Compliance**: Legal compliance requirements
