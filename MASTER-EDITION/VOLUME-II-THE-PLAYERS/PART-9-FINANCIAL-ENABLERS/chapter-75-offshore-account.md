# Chapter 75: The "Offshore" Account

## Secrecy Jurisdictions

Offshore accounts provide secrecy for drug money:

**Offshore Jurisdictions:**
- **Tax Havens**: Countries with low or no taxes
- **Secrecy Laws**: Laws protecting account holder privacy
- **Banking Secrecy**: Strict banking secrecy regulations
- **Corporate Secrecy**: Anonymous corporate structures
- **Trust Structures**: Trust structures hiding ownership
- **Asset Protection**: Protection from legal claims

**Major Offshore Centers:**
- **Switzerland**: Traditional banking secrecy
- **Cayman Islands**: Major offshore financial center
- **British Virgin Islands**: Popular for shell companies
- **Panama**: Offshore banking and corporate services
- **Singapore**: Asian offshore financial center
- **Luxembourg**: European offshore financial center

**Services Provided:**
- **Anonymous Accounts**: Accounts with minimal identification
- **Shell Companies**: Companies with hidden ownership
- **Trust Services**: Trust structures hiding ownership
- **Investment Services**: Investment management services
- **Tax Planning**: Tax planning and minimisation
- **Asset Protection**: Protection from legal claims

**Regulatory Pressure:**
- **International Pressure**: International pressure for transparency
- **Automatic Exchange**: Automatic exchange of tax information
- **Beneficial Ownership**: Requirements to disclose beneficial ownership
- **Blacklists**: Blacklists of non-cooperative jurisdictions
- **Sanctions**: Sanctions on jurisdictions facilitating money laundering
- **Reform**: Some jurisdictions reforming to maintain access

**The Offshore Account's Role:**
- **Secrecy**: Provides secrecy for drug money
- **Asset Protection**: Protects assets from seizure
- **Tax Evasion**: Facilitates tax evasion
- **Complexity**: Adds complexity to money laundering
- **International Scope**: Enables international money movement
