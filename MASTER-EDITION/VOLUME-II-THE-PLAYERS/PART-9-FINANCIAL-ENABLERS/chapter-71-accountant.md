# Chapter 71: The Accountant

## Professional Services for Money Laundering

Accountants provide critical services for money laundering:

**Services Provided:**
- **Bookkeeping**: Recording financial transactions
- **Tax Planning**: Structuring transactions to minimise taxes
- **Financial Statements**: Preparing financial statements
- **Audit Services**: Auditing financial records
- **Consulting**: Financial consulting services
- **Shell Company Management**: Managing shell companies for money laundering

**Money Laundering Techniques:**
- **Complex Structures**: Creating complex corporate structures
- **Offshore Accounts**: Moving money to offshore jurisdictions
- **Trust Structures**: Creating trusts to hide ownership
- **Loan Structures**: Creating fake loans to move money
- **Investment Portfolios**: Investing drug money in legitimate assets
- **Tax Evasion**: Evading taxes on illegal income

**Professional Ethics:**
- **Ethical Obligations**: Professional ethical obligations
- **Legal Requirements**: Legal requirements to report suspicious activity
- **Due Diligence**: Requirements to verify client information
- **Professional Standards**: Must adhere to professional standards
- **Legal Liability**: Can be held liable for facilitating criminal activities

**The Accountant's Perspective:**
- **Professional Service**: Providing professional services
- **Client Confidentiality**: Commitment to client confidentiality
- **Ethical Dilemmas**: Ethical dilemmas about client activities
- **Risk Management**: Managing professional and legal risk
- **Financial Expertise**: Utilising financial expertise
