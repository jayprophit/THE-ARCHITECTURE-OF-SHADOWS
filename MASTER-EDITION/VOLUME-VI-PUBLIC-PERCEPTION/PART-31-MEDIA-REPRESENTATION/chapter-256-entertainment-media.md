# Chapter 256: Entertainment Media

## Drugs in Movies and Television

Entertainment media significantly influences perceptions of drugs:

**Portrayal Patterns:**
- **Glamorisation**: Glamorisation of drug use
- **Dramatisation**: Dramatic portrayal of addiction
- **Comedy**: Comedic portrayal of drug use
- **Tragedy**: Tragic portrayal of addiction
- **Recovery**: Stories of recovery and redemption
- **Consequences**: Depiction of drug consequences

**Movie Portrayals:**
- **Drug Movies**: Movies focused on drug use
- **Character Types**: Stereotypical drug user characters
- **Drug Trade**: Portrayal of drug trade
- **Addiction**: Portrayal of addiction
- **Treatment**: Portrayal of treatment
- **Recovery**: Portrayal of recovery

**Television Portrayals:**
- **Drama Series**: Drug storylines in drama series
- **Comedy Series**: Drug use in comedy series
- **Documentary Series**: Documentary series on drug issues
- **Reality TV**: Reality TV about addiction
- **News Programs**: Television news coverage
- **Advertising**: Television advertising

**Stereotypes:**
- **Drug User Stereotypes**: Stereotypical portrayals of drug users
- **Dealer Stereotypes**: Stereotypical drug dealer portrayals
- **Addict Stereotypes**: Stereotypical addict characters
- **Recovery Stereotypes**: Stereotypical recovery portrayals
- **Racial Stereotypes**: Racial stereotypes in drug portrayals
- **Gender Stereotypes**: Gender stereotypes in drug portrayals

**Impact on Perception:**
- **Normalization**: Normalization of drug use
- **Stigma**: Reinforcement or reduction of stigma
- **Understanding**: Public understanding of addiction
- **Fear**: Creation of fear about drugs
- **Glamour**: Glamour associated with drug use
- **Reality**: Distortion of reality

**The Entertainment Media Reality:**
- **Influential**: Highly influential on perceptions
- **Varied**: Varied portrayals across genres
- **Stereotypical**: Often stereotypical portrayals
- **Glamorising**: Sometimes glamorising drug use
- **Evolving**: Evolving portrayals over time
- **Responsibility**: Industry responsibility in portrayal
