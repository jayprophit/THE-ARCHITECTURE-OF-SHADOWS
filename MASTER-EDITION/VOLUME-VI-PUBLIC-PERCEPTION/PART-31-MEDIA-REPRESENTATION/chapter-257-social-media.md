# Chapter 257: Social Media

## Drug Discourse on Social Platforms

Social media has transformed discourse about drugs:

**Platform Types:**
- **Facebook**: Drug-related content on Facebook
- **Twitter/X**: Drug discussions on Twitter/X
- **Instagram**: Visual drug content on Instagram
- **TikTok**: Drug content on TikTok
- **YouTube**: Drug-related videos on YouTube
- **Reddit**: Drug discussions on Reddit

**User-Generated Content:**
- **Personal Stories**: Personal stories of drug use and recovery
- **Experience Sharing**: Sharing drug experiences
- **Advice**: Advice about drug use
- **Community Building**: Building online drug communities
- **Support**: Peer support through social media
- **Misinformation**: Misinformation about drugs

**Influencer Culture:**
- **Drug Influencers**: Influencers discussing drug use
- **Wellness Influencers**: Wellness influencers discussing psychedelics
- **Recovery Influencers**: Recovery influencers sharing stories
- **Medical Influencers**: Medical professionals discussing drugs
- **Celebrity Influence**: Celebrity posts about drugs
- **Sponsorship**: Sponsorship of drug-related content

**Drug Marketing:**
- **Cannabis Marketing**: Cannabis marketing on social media
- **Psychedelic Marketing**: Marketing of psychedelic products
- **Supplement Marketing**: Marketing of nootropics and supplements
- **Influencer Marketing**: Influencer marketing of drug products
- **Targeted Advertising**: Targeted advertising for drug products
- **Regulatory Issues**: Regulatory issues with drug marketing

**Misinformation and Harm:**
- **Dangerous Advice**: Dangerous advice about drug use
- **Fake Drugs**: Sales of fake or dangerous substances
- **Self-Harm Content**: Content promoting self-harm
- **Eating Disorders**: Content promoting eating disorders
- **Challenge Videos**: Dangerous drug challenge videos
- **Platform Response**: Platform response to harmful content

**The Social Media Reality:**
- **Transformative**: Transformed drug discourse
- **Unregulated**: Largely unregulated space
- **Mixed**: Mix of helpful and harmful content
- **Influential**: Highly influential on perceptions
- **Challenging**: Challenging to regulate
- **Evolving**: Evolving platform policies
