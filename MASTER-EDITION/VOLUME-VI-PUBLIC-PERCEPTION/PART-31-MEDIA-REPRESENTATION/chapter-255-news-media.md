# Chapter 255: News Media Coverage

## News Reporting on Drug Issues

News media coverage shapes public understanding of drug issues:

**Coverage Patterns:**
- **Crime Focus**: Emphasis on drug-related crime
- **Sensationalism**: Sensationalist coverage of drug issues
- **Moral Panic**: Moral panic framing
- **Individual Stories**: Individual addiction stories
- **Policy Debates**: Coverage of policy debates
- **Expert Sources**: Use of expert sources

**Framing of Drug Issues:**
- **Criminal Justice Frame**: Criminal justice framing of drug issues
- **Public Health Frame**: Public health framing
- **Social Problem Frame**: Social problem framing
- **Moral Frame**: Moral framing of drug use
- **Economic Frame**: Economic framing
- **Political Frame**: Political framing

**Bias in Coverage:**
- **Racial Bias**: Racial bias in drug coverage
- **Class Bias**: Class bias in coverage
- **Ideological Bias**: Ideological bias in coverage
- **Sensational Bias**: Bias toward sensational stories
- **Conflict Bias**: Bias toward conflict stories
- **Access Bias**: Bias based on source access

**Impact on Public Opinion:**
- **Public Perception**: Influence on public perception
- **Policy Support**: Influence on policy support
- **Stigma**: Reinforcement of stigma
- **Fear**: Creation of fear about drugs
- **Understanding**: Public understanding of complex issues
- **Agenda Setting**: Setting the public agenda

**Responsible Journalism:**
- **Accurate Reporting**: Accurate and balanced reporting
- **Context**: Providing context for drug issues
- **Expert Sources**: Use of qualified expert sources
- **Avoiding Sensationalism**: Avoiding sensationalism
- **Humanizing**: Humanizing people who use drugs
- **Evidence-Based**: Evidence-based reporting

**The News Media Reality:**
- **Influential**: Highly influential on public opinion
- **Problematic**: Often problematic coverage
- **Sensationalist**: Tendency toward sensationalism
- **Biased**: Biased coverage patterns
- **Improving**: Some improvement in coverage quality
- **Responsibility**: Need for responsible journalism
