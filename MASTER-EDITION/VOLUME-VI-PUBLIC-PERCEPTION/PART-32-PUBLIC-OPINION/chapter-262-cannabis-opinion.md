- **Medical Cannabis**: Growing support for medical cannabis
- **Recreational Legalisation**: Growing support for recreational legalisation
- **Generational Shift**: Generational differences in attitudes
- **Political Shift**: Political shift on cannabis policy
- **Global Trends**: Global trends in cannabis opinion

- **Medical Cannabis**: Strong support for medical cannabis
