# Chapter 383: Appendix C - Statistics

## Key Statistics on Drugs and Drug Policy

**Global Drug Use**
- **Estimated Users**: 269 million people used drugs in 2018 (UNODC)
- **Cannabis Users**: 192 million cannabis users globally
- **Opioid Users**: 58 million opioid users globally
- **Amphetamine Users**: 30 million amphetamine users
- **Cocaine Users**: 20 million cocaine users
- **Ecstasy Users**: 21 million ecstasy users

**Drug-Related Deaths**
- **Global Deaths**: 585,000 drug-related deaths in 2017
- **Overdose Deaths**: 167,750 overdose deaths in US in 2021
- **Opioid Deaths**: Over 100,000 opioid deaths in US in 2021
- **HIV Deaths**: 680,000 HIV deaths among people who inject drugs

**Drug Trade Economics**
- **Global Drug Trade**: Estimated $400-650 billion annually
- **US Drug Spending**: $150 billion annually on illegal drugs
- **Cocaine Trade**: $84 billion annual trade
- **Heroin Trade**: $68 billion annual trade
- **Cannabis Trade**: $141 billion annual trade

**Enforcement Statistics**
- **Global Drug Seizures**: 2,500 tons of drugs seized annually
- **US Drug Arrests**: 1.5 million drug arrests annually
- **Incarceration**: Over 10 million people incarcerated for drugs globally
- **US Prison Population**: 45% of US prisoners incarcerated for drug offenses

**Treatment Statistics**
- **Global Treatment Gap**: Only 1 in 7 people with drug use disorders receive treatment
- **US Treatment**: 4 million people received treatment in 2019
- **Medication-Assisted Treatment**: Only 10% of opioid users receive MAT
- **Relapse Rates**: 40-60% relapse rate for substance use disorders

**Note**: Statistics are approximate and vary by source and year.
