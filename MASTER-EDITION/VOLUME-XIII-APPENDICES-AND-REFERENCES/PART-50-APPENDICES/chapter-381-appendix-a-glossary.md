# Chapter 381: Appendix A - Glossary

## Glossary of Terms

**A**
- **Addiction**: Chronic condition of drug seeking and use
- **Adverse Effect**: Unwanted reaction to a substance
- **Ayahuasca**: Traditional Amazonian psychoactive brew

**B**
- **Black Budget**: Classified government budget
- **Border Security**: Measures to secure borders
- **Bribery**: Offering money to influence actions

**C**
- **Cannabis**: Psychoactive plant species
- **Cartel**: Criminal organization in drug trade
- **CIA**: Central Intelligence Agency
- **Compartmentalization**: Division of information for security

**D**
- **Decriminalisation**: Removal of criminal penalties
- **Deep State**: Hidden government apparatus
- **Drug Trafficking**: Illegal trade in controlled substances

**E**
- **Entheogen**: Psychoactive substance used in religious context
- **Epidemic**: Widespread occurrence of disease
- **Evidence-Based**: Based on scientific evidence

**F**
- **Front Organization**: Cover for covert activities
- **Fentanyl**: Potent synthetic opioid
- **Five Eyes**: Intelligence alliance

**H**
- **Harm Reduction**: Policies reducing drug harm
- **Heroin**: Semi-synthetic opioid
- **HIV**: Human immunodeficiency virus

**I**
- **Intelligence**: Information gathering
- **Interdiction**: Prevention of illegal activities
- **Illicit**: Illegal or forbidden

**L**
- **Legalisation**: Making legal what was illegal
- **Lobbying**: Attempting to influence legislators
- **Law Enforcement**: Agencies enforcing laws

**M**
- **Money Laundering**: Concealing origins of illegal money
- **MDMA**: Synthetic psychoactive substance
- **Methamphetamine**: Synthetic stimulant

**N**
- **Narcotic**: Substance affecting mood or behavior
- **NPS**: Novel Psychoactive Substances
- **Naloxone**: Opioid overdose reversal medication

**O**
- **Opioid**: Substance acting on opioid receptors
- **Overdose**: Excessive dose causing harm
- **Opium**: Narcotic derived from poppy

**P**
- **Psychedelic**: Mind-manifesting substance
- **Prohibition**: Legal ban on substances
- **Precursor**: Chemical used to make another substance

**S**
- **Shadow System**: Hidden governance structure
- **Stigma**: Social disgrace or shame
- **Supply Chain**: Network of production and distribution

**T**
- **Treatment**: Medical care for addiction
- **Trafficking**: Illegal trade
- **Tolerance**: Reduced response to substance

**Note**: This is a partial glossary. Full glossary would be extensive.
