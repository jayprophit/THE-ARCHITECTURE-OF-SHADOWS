# Chapter 385: Appendix E - Key Documents

## Key Documents in Drug Policy

**International Treaties**
- **1961 Single Convention**: UN Single Convention on Narcotic Drugs
- **1971 Convention**: UN Convention on Psychotropic Substances
- **1988 Convention**: UN Convention Against Illicit Traffic
- **Universal Declaration**: Universal Declaration of Human Rights

**US Legislation**
- **Harrison Act (1914)**: Harrison Narcotics Tax Act
- **CSA (1970)**: Controlled Substances Act
- **Anti-Drug Abuse Act (1986)**: Mandatory minimum sentencing
- **Comprehensive Drug Abuse Prevention and Control Act (1970)**

**Key Reports**
- **LaGuardia Report (1944)**: New York Mayor's Committee on Marijuana
- **Shafer Commission (1972)**: National Commission on Marihuana and Drug Abuse
- **Le Dain Commission (1973)**: Canadian Commission of Inquiry into the Non-Medical Use of Drugs
- **Various UNODC Reports**: Annual World Drug Reports

**Court Decisions**
- **Gonzales v. Raich (2005)**: US Supreme Court medical cannabis case
- **Various State Cases**: State-level court decisions on drug policy
- **International Court Cases**: International drug-related cases

**Policy Documents**
- **National Drug Control Strategies**: Annual US drug strategies
- **EU Drug Strategies**: European Union Drug Strategies
- **Various National Policies**: National drug policies worldwide

**Note**: This is a selective list of key documents.
