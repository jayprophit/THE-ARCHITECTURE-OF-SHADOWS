# Chapter 233: Cannabis Legalisation

## Models and Experiences of Cannabis Legalisation

Cannabis legalisation provides real-world experience with regulated drug markets:

**Legalisation Models:**
- **Uruguay**: First country to legalise cannabis nationally
- **Canada**: Legal cannabis market with federal regulation
- **US States**: State-level legalisation in multiple states
- **Thailand**: Recent cannabis legalisation
- **Other Countries**: Emerging legalisation efforts
- **Model Variation**: Variation in legalisation models

**Uruguay Model:**
- **State Control**: State-controlled cannabis market
- **Licensed Production**: Licensed cannabis production
- **Pharmacy Sales**: Sales through pharmacies
- **Cannabis Clubs**: Cannabis social clubs
- **Resident Registration**: Resident registration for purchase
- **Goals**: Goals of reducing illicit market

**Canada Model:**
- **Federal Legalisation**: Federal legalisation framework
- **Provincial Regulation**: Provincial regulation of distribution
- **Private Retail**: Private retail sales
- **Quality Control**: Strict quality control
- **Taxation**: Cannabis taxation
- **Public Education**: Public education about cannabis

**US State Models:**
- **Colorado**: First state to legalise adult-use cannabis
- **Washington**: Washington state legalisation model
- **California**: California's large legal market
- **Other States**: Various state approaches
- **Federal Conflict**: Conflict with federal prohibition
- **State Variation**: Variation between states

**Outcomes and Lessons:**
- **Market Development**: Development of legal markets
- **Illicit Market Persistence**: Persistence of illicit market
- **Tax Revenue**: Tax revenue generation
- **Public Health**: Public health outcomes
- **Youth Use**: Impact on youth cannabis use
- **Law Enforcement**: Changes in law enforcement

**The Cannabis Legalisation Reality:**
- **Successful**: Generally successful implementation
- **Learning Curve**: Steep learning curve
- **Regulatory Challenges**: Ongoing regulatory challenges
- **Market Evolution**: Markets continue to evolve
- **Evidence-Based**: Growing evidence base
- **Global Influence**: Influencing global policy
