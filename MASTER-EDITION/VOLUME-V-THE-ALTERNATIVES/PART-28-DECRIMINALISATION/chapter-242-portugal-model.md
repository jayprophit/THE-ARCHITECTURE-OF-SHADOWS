# Chapter 242: The Portugal Model

## Portugal's Decriminalisation Experience

Portugal's decriminalisation model is the most studied example:

**Background:**
- **2001 Reform**: 2001 decriminalisation of all drugs
- **Drug Crisis**: Severe drug crisis before reform
- **HIV Epidemic**: High HIV rates among injection drug users
- **Overdose Crisis**: High overdose rates
- **Prison Overcrowding**: Prison overcrowding from drug offenses
- **Policy Shift**: Shift from criminal justice to public health approach

**Decriminalisation Framework:**
- **All Drugs**: Decriminalisation of all illicit drugs
- **Possession Thresholds**: Quantity thresholds for personal use
- **Civil Penalties**: Civil instead of criminal penalties
- **Dissuasion Commissions**: Commissions to handle drug offenses
- **Treatment Referral**: Referral to treatment instead of prosecution
- **Focus on Health**: Public health focus over punishment

**Outcomes:**
- **Drug Use**: No significant increase in drug use
- **Overdose Deaths**: Dramatic reduction in overdose deaths
- **HIV/AIDS**: Dramatic reduction in HIV transmission
- **Incarceration**: Dramatic reduction in drug-related incarceration
- **Treatment Access**: Increased access to treatment
- **Stigma**: Reduced stigma for drug users

**Implementation Challenges:**
- **Political Opposition**: Initial political opposition
- **Public Concerns**: Public concerns about increased use
- **International Pressure**: Pressure from international drug control bodies
- **Resource Allocation**: Allocation of resources to treatment
- **Cultural Change**: Cultural change in attitudes toward drugs
- **Adaptation**: Adaptation of policies over time

**Lessons Learned:**
- **Evidence-Based**: Evidence-based policy works
- **Treatment Focus**: Treatment focus reduces harm
- **Stigma Reduction**: Stigma reduction improves outcomes
- **Resource Investment**: Investment in treatment essential
- **Political Courage**: Political courage needed for reform
- **Long-Term Commitment**: Long-term commitment to approach

**The Portugal Reality:**
- **Success**: Generally considered successful model
- **Evidence-Based**: Strong evidence base for outcomes
- **Influential**: Influential in global drug policy debates
- **Adaptable**: Model adaptable to other contexts
- **Not Perfect**: Not perfect, but significant improvement
- **Model**: Model for other countries considering reform
