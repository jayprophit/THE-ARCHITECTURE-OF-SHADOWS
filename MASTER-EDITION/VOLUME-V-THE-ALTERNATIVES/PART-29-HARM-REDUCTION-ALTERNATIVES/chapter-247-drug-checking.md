# Chapter 247: Drug Checking Services

## Testing Drug Substances for Safety

Drug checking services test substances to protect users from dangerous adulterants:

**Service Types:**
- **Laboratory Testing**: Laboratory analysis of substances
- **On-Site Testing**: On-site testing at events
- **Mobile Testing**: Mobile drug checking services
- **Mail-In Services**: Mail-in testing services
- **Community-Based**: Community-based checking programs
- **Festival Services**: Drug checking at festivals and events

**Testing Methods:**
- **FTIR Spectroscopy**: Fourier-transform infrared spectroscopy
- **Raman Spectroscopy**: Raman spectroscopy analysis
- **Colorimetric Tests**: Colorimetric reagent tests
- **GC-MS**: Gas chromatography-mass spectrometry
- **LC-MS**: Liquid chromatography-mass spectrometry
- **Immunoassay**: Immunoassay tests for specific substances

**Substances Detected:**
- **Fentanyl**: Detection of fentanyl and analogues
- **Adulterants**: Detection of dangerous adulterants
- **Purity**: Assessment of substance purity
- **Identification**: Identification of unknown substances
- **Novel Psychoactive Substances**: Detection of NPS
- **Contaminants**: Detection of harmful contaminants

**Benefits:**
- **Overdose Prevention**: Prevention of overdose from adulterated substances
- **Harm Reduction**: Reduced harm from dangerous substances
- **Informed Use**: Informed decision-making by users
- **Market Influence**: Influence on drug market quality
- **Public Health**: Public health benefits
- **Data Collection**: Collection of drug market data

**Challenges:**
- **Legal Barriers**: Legal barriers to drug checking
- **Funding**: Funding challenges for services
- **Liability**: Liability concerns for service providers
- **Accuracy**: Accuracy and limitations of testing
- **Access**: Limited access to services
- **Stigma**: Stigma affecting service use

**The Drug Checking Reality:**
- **Effective**: Effective at preventing harm
- **Life-Saving**: Life-saving intervention
- **Controversial**: Controversial due to legal concerns
- **Expanding**: Expanding implementation
- **Evidence-Based**: Evidence-based harm reduction
- **Essential**: Essential service for user safety
