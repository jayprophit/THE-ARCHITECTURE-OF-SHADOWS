- **Civil Disobedience**: Civil disobedience against unjust laws
- **Conscious Non-Compliance**: Conscious non-compliance
- **Passive Resistance**: Passive resistance techniques
- **Strategic Non-Cooperation**: Strategic non-cooperation

**Economic Non-Cooperation:**

- **Divestment**: Divestment from harmful systems
- **Local Economy**: Support of local economy
- **Alternative Currencies**: Use of alternative currencies
- **Reduced Consumption**: Reduced consumption

- **Alternative Politics**: Alternative political engagement
- **Local Governance**: Focus on local governance

- **Cultural Non-Participation**: Non-participation in controlled culture
- **Social Norm Resistance**: Resistance to social norms
- **Alternative Social Structures**: Alternative social structures
- **Community Autonomy**: Community autonomy
- **Cultural Creation**: Creation of alternative culture

**The Non-Cooperation Reality:**
